# Manta Ray Bay

A curated entry representing manta encounter diving in Yap's sheltered waters; the named dive sites in this area are the Mi'l Channel and Goofnuw Channel cleaning stations

## Overview
"Manta Ray Bay" is the name of Yap's primary dive resort (Manta Ray Bay Resort) and the general bay area in front of it, but no independently documented dive site by this exact name has been confirmed in published dive operator listings or diver trip reports. The actual named manta diving sites in Yap are: the Mi'l Channel cleaning stations (new and old) and Tzimoulis Ridge on the northwest side of the island; and the Goofnuw Channel sites (Valley of the Rays / Stammtisch) on the northeast side. This entry is retained as a placeholder for the broader bay area manta encounters but should be considered unverified as a distinct named dive site.

## Notes for Researchers
Yap's confirmed manta diving sites include:
- **Mi'l Channel**: Two cleaning stations (new ~5m; old ~18m) plus Tzimoulis Ridge (~9m). Active November-May primarily.
- **Goofnuw Channel (Valley of the Rays)**: Three cleaning stations (Merry-Go-Round at 20m, Car Wash, Manta Rock). Active summer months.
- **Stammtisch**: Cleaning station at ~4.6m inside the lagoon.
