## Tices Shoal

Tices Shoal is a reef dive site in New Jersey Shore, North America.

## Overview

Tices Shoal is a shallow sandbar in the middle of Barnegat Bay, long famous as a summer boating and swimming spot rather than a traditional dive site, with depths maxing out at about 3 meters over rippled sand and scattered shell bottom. The shoal's warm, calm water and easy boat access make it a popular snorkeling and introductory dive location for New Jersey families, where horseshoe crabs, hermit crabs, bay scallops, and flounder are regular encounters in season. It offers none of the dramatic wreck diving New Jersey is known for offshore, but serves as a gentle entry point to the state's underwater environment.
