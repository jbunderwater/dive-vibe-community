## Goddamahagale dive site

Locally known as Goda Maha Gala, a rocky reef roughly 6-7km offshore from Negombo — a boat dive, not the shore dive this page previously described.

## Overview

Goddamahagale — known locally as Goda Maha Gala — is a rocky reef about 6 to 7 kilometers off Negombo on Sri Lanka's west coast. Multiple Negombo-area dive operators list it as one of the region's standard sites, reachable only by boat given the distance from shore (the previous "shore entry" listing on this page was incorrect). Depths across the site run 12-15 meters. Some operator descriptions characterize the area as having small cave/swim-through features among the rock, though we have kept the site classified as a reef here since the majority of source descriptions emphasize rock-and-coral structure rather than a distinct cave system.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Sri Lanka, Asia
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 15 meters

## Marine Life

Operators consistently report snappers, lobsters, black coral and moray eels at this specific site.
