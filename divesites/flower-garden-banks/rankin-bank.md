## Rankin Bank

Rankin Bank is a deep offshore bank in the Flower Garden Banks National Marine Sanctuary complex, part of the 2021 sanctuary expansion, with a minimum depth of approximately 50 meters that places it beyond standard recreational dive limits.

## Overview

Rankin Bank lies approximately 124 miles (200 km) from shore and 9.3 miles east of East Flower Garden Bank. Named after John L. Rankin, the first Gulf Region Director of the Minerals Management Service (now BOEM), it was added to the sanctuary in the January 2021 expansion. NOAA reports Rankin Bank's depth range as 164–571 feet (50–174 meters), putting its shallowest reef habitat at approximately 50 meters — beyond the 130-foot (39.6-meter) recreational dive limit. Rankin sits just north of 28 Fathom Bank, separated by a 1,000-meter-wide trough reaching 174 meters. The two banks connect structurally to MacNeil Bank and East Flower Garden Bank via underwater ridges, and they share a Habitat Area of Particular Concern (HAPC) designation with Bright Bank and connecting patch reefs. NOAA does not list Rankin Bank among the seven sanctuary banks with reef habitat within recreational dive limits.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Gulf of Mexico, 124 miles offshore (27.68°N, 93.58°W)
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Advanced
- **Minimum Depth**: 50 meters (164 feet)
- **Typical Visibility**: 15–30 meters, estimated
- **Water Temperature**: 20–21°C (68–70°F) at depth in winter; warmer at surface
- **Current**: Variable offshore conditions
- **Best Time**: Not regularly visited by recreational divers

## Marine Life

NOAA documents black corals, octocorals, algae, sponges, stony corals, and invertebrates at Rankin and 28 Fathom Banks. Deeper zones feature groupers inhabiting whip coral fields. The reef community is mesophotic in character given the minimum 50-meter depth. No site-specific recreational dive reports were found in the sources reviewed.

## Dive Profile

With a minimum depth of 50 meters, Rankin Bank is a technical/mixed-gas diving site, not accessible for standard recreational open-circuit air diving. Exploration is primarily conducted by researchers using rebreathers, remotely operated vehicles, or manned submersibles. Divers interested in this bank should contact sanctuary staff or specialized technical operators.

## Entry and Exit

Access by charter vessel only; anchoring is prohibited throughout the sanctuary. The site is 124 miles offshore. No mooring buoys are confirmed for Rankin Bank by NOAA (buoys are confirmed at Stetson, East and West Flower Garden, Bright, Geyer, and Rezak Banks). Contact Texas Caribbean Charters or sanctuary staff for any access information.

## Safety Considerations

Rankin Bank is beyond recreational dive limits at 50 meters minimum depth. Technical diving training, mixed gas, and decompression capability are required. At 124 miles offshore, emergency response is a multi-hour operation. This site is not suitable for recreational divers.
