## Boulders

Large coral-encrusted boulders on the southwestern tip of Nusa Kode creating habitat for giant potato cod, Malabar groupers, bamboo sharks, and schools of giant trevally and fusiliers. Coleman shrimp on fire urchins and rare paddleflap scorpionfish (Rhinopia) attract macro photographers. Also a popular night dive.

## Site Information

- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Coral reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 30 meters
