## Golden Passage

A high-energy drift dive through the narrow channel between Gili Lawa Darat and Komodo Island. Tidal currents reaching several knots sweep divers past volcanic rock walls covered in hard and soft corals. Schools of trevally, whitetip and blacktip reef sharks, hawksbill turtles, and occasional manta rays gather in the nutrient-rich flow.

## Site Information

- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Drift dive
- **Difficulty Level**: Advanced
- **Maximum Depth**: 25 meters
