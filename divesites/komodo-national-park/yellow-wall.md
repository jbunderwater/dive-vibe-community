## Yellow Wall

A dramatic wall along the east coast of Nusa Kode named for the profusion of bright yellow soft corals (Tubastrea) and robust sea cucumbers. Crevices harbor nudibranchs and ladybug amphipods, with clown triggerfish, fairy basslets, and occasional sharks and mantas cruising past. Best dived in the afternoon for optimal light. Medium to strong currents.

## Site Information

- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Wall dive
- **Difficulty Level**: Advanced
- **Maximum Depth**: 40 meters
