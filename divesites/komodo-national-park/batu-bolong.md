## Batu Bolong

An exposed rock pinnacle rising from over 70m in the center of Lintah Strait. Strong currents hit alternating sides depending on tide. Every inch of the pinnacle is covered in healthy coral, hosting enormous densities of anthias, reef sharks, giant trevally, dogtooth tuna, and napoleon wrasse. Regularly ranked among the world's top dive sites.

## Site Information

- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Pinnacle/seamount
- **Difficulty Level**: Advanced
- **Maximum Depth**: 30 meters
