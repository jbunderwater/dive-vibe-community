## Castle Rock

A large underwater seamount north of Gili Lawa Laut with its peak just meters below the surface. Blanketed in soft corals and sea fans hosting pygmy seahorses. Medium to very strong currents draw huge schools of fusiliers, whitetip and grey reef sharks, giant trevally, napoleon wrasse, and occasional eagle rays. Widely considered one of Komodo's best dive sites.

## Site Information

- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Pinnacle/seamount
- **Difficulty Level**: Advanced
- **Maximum Depth**: 35 meters
