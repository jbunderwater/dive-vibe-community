## Crinoid Wall

A colorful wall in a small cove near the southern entrance to Loh Dasami at Nusa Kode, almost directly opposite Yellow Wall. Crevices hold sleeping slipper lobsters, cleaner shrimp, decorator crabs, red sea apples, and a great variety of tunicates and soft corals. An excellent slow-paced macro dive best done in the morning.

## Site Information

- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Wall dive
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 25 meters
