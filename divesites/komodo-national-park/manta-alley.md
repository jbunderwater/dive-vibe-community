## Manta Alley

A rocky channel at the far southern tip of Komodo Island where manta rays aggregate at cleaning stations, with sightings of 20+ mantas on a single dive not uncommon during peak season (November to March). Strong currents and cold thermoclines make this a challenging but unforgettable dive. Also home to giant trevallies, sweetlips, and occasional mobula rays.

## Site Information

- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Drift dive
- **Difficulty Level**: Advanced
- **Maximum Depth**: 40 meters
