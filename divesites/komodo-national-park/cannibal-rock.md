## Cannibal Rock

A small seamount in Loh Dasami Bay at Nusa Kode, carpeted in dense soft corals, crinoids, and sponges. Widely regarded as one of Indonesia's premier macro and critter diving sites. Home to an extraordinary density of invertebrate life including nudibranchs, sea apples, and crinoid shrimp, with occasional manta rays and reef sharks passing by.

## Site Information

- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Muck dive
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 35 meters
