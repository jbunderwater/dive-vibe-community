Disko Bay rocky reef sites of this type feature granite boulders and ledges supporting cold-water kelp forests, dense sea anemone colonies, sea urchins, starfish, sea cucumbers, and typical Arctic cold-water invertebrates. Icebergs calved from the Ilulissat Icefjord (a UNESCO World Heritage Site approximately 50 km south) drift through Disko Bay and are a characteristic feature of the region.
