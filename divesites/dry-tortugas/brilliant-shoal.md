## Entry and Exit

Access is by private or charter boat only — the park is 70 miles from Key West. No air facilities exist in the park; arrive with full tanks. The Yankee Freedom III ferry cannot carry compressed tanks.

## Safety Considerations

The Dry Tortugas offer no onsite emergency dive support; the nearest hyperbaric chamber is in Key West. No cell service in the park — carry a marine VHF radio. Dive with a buddy and display a dive flag.
