## Lakeside

Lakeside is a reef dive site in Ningaloo Reef, Oceania.

## Overview

Lakeside is a sheltered snorkel and beginner dive site within a sanctuary zone of Ningaloo Marine Park, located approximately 52 km north of Exmouth near the Milyering Discovery Centre. The site features large coral bommies — referred to locally as "Coral Bombies" — in 1-5 metres of water, accessible directly from the beach. Confirmed fish species include banded humbugs, harlequin filefish, Hawaiian triggerfish, blackspot snapper, and various anemone species. The site's shallow depth (1-5 metres, not 10 metres as recorded in metadata) and easy entry make it suitable for beginners. Claims of wobbegong sharks at this specific site are not corroborated by current sources. Ningaloo Reef's waters support diverse marine life including sea turtles, but no site-specific confirmation of "guaranteed" turtle encounters at Lakeside was found.
