## Hottest Sparrow

Hottest Sparrow is a wall dive site in Roatán, Caribbean.

## Overview

Hottest Sparrow is the easternmost dive site on the Roatan Marine Park map, located at the far eastern end of Roatan's south coast barrier reef. While individual published information is limited, this remote position means the site sees far less diver traffic than the popular West End and Sandy Bay areas, potentially offering more pristine reef conditions.
