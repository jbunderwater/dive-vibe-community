## Ocean Groove

Ocean Groove is a wall dive site in Roatán, Caribbean.

## Overview

Ocean Groove occupies a stretch of the West End wall where subtle grooves and channels cut into the reef face, creating natural swim-throughs and sheltered passages. These formations concentrate marine life, with schools of blue-striped grunts gathering in the recesses and midnight parrotfish scraping algae from the coral. The wall descends from a shallow reef top at around 12 meters, and the grooves funnel divers naturally along the wall face. Tube sponges in shades of lavender and gray rise from the wall like chimneys, and queen angelfish are a frequent sight along this section of the corridor.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Roatán, Caribbean
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Wall dive
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 24 meters
