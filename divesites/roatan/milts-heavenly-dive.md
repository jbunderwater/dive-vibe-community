## Milt's Heavenly Dive

Milt's Heavenly Dive is a wall dive site in Roatán, Caribbean.

## Overview

Milt's Heavenly Dive is a Roatan Marine Park mooring site along the barrier reef east of Sandy Bay. While specific diving descriptions for this site are not widely published, the eastern reef corridor offers excellent wall diving on healthy coral formations.
