## Mb's Wall

Mb's Wall is a wall dive site in Roatán, Caribbean.

## Overview

Mb's Wall is a Roatan Marine Park mooring site positioned along the West End wall where the reef crest gives way to a vertical face dropping into deep water. Large barrel sponges anchor themselves to the wall at various depths, their wide openings creating resting spots for blennies and gobies. Sea fans and plume-like gorgonians wave gently in the current, attracting flamingo tongue snails. Divers working along the wall at intermediate depths are likely to encounter groupers holding station near cleaning stations and spotted eagle rays making their characteristic gliding passes. Visibility here is typically excellent, often exceeding 30 meters.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Roatán, Caribbean
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Wall dive
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 30 meters
