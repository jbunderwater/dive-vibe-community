## Kristi's Cove

Kristi's Cove is a wall dive site in Roatán, Caribbean.

## Overview

Kristi's Cove is a donor-named RMP mooring in the Sandy Bay area, where the reef forms a slight indentation along the wall. This sheltered topography creates a calm pocket that attracts marine life, with schools of sergeant majors and brown chromis hovering above the reef crest. The wall face features large tube sponges and deep-water sea fans, and green sea turtles are commonly seen in the shallows. Visibility is generally 20 to 40 meters, with water temperatures from 26 to 29 degrees Celsius.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Roatán, Caribbean
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Wall dive
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 27 meters
