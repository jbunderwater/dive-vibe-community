## シル東 (Shiru Higashi (Shiru East))

Located on the east side of Zamami Island, more exposed to open ocean conditions than the sheltered western sites. The east-side exposure means this site may have more current and larger marine life than the calm inner-reef points.

## Overview

Part of Kerama Shoto National Park, known for 'Kerama Blue' water with 20-50 metre visibility and abundant sea turtle encounters. Located on the east side of Zamami Island, more exposed to open ocean conditions than the sheltered western sites. The east-side exposure means this site may have more current and larger marine life than the calm inner-reef points.

## Site Information

- **Location**: East Zamami Island, Kerama Islands, Okinawa Prefecture, Japan
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Coral reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 20 meters
- **Typical Visibility**: 20-50 metres ('Kerama Blue')
- **Water Temperature**: 22-29C
- **Best Time**: Year-round; calmest seas March to November

## Marine Life

Reef fish, corals. The more exposed position may attract larger pelagic visitors compared to sheltered inner-reef sites.
