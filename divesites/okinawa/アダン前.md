## アダン前 (Adan Mae (Front of Pandanus))

Named after the Pandanus (Adan) trees that line the shore at this location. A typical Kerama inner-reef site with coral gardens and sandy bottom. The sheltered position means calm conditions year-round.

## Overview

Part of Kerama Shoto National Park, known for 'Kerama Blue' water with 20-50 metre visibility and abundant sea turtle encounters. Named after the Pandanus (Adan) trees that line the shore at this location. A typical Kerama inner-reef site with coral gardens and sandy bottom. The sheltered position means calm conditions year-round.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Zamami/Aka area, Kerama Islands, Okinawa Prefecture, Japan
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Coral reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Beginner
- **Maximum Depth**: 18 meters
- **Typical Visibility**: 20-50 metres ('Kerama Blue')
- **Water Temperature**: 22-29C
- **Best Time**: Year-round; calmest seas March to November

## Marine Life

Table corals, reef fish, damselfish, sea turtles (area-typical). The coral gardens host standard Kerama reef communities.
