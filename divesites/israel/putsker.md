A dive site named after the Putsker Diving Club — one of the main dive operations based in Nahariya, northern Israel.

## Overview

"Putsker" is the name of a well-established diving club headquartered in Nahariya (located at HaMaapilim 5, Nahariya), which operates dives across the northern Israeli Mediterranean coast from Haifa Bay to Rosh Hanikra. This site appears to be named after the club, suggesting it may be a "house reef" or frequently-visited site associated with the Putsker Diving Club's operations in the Nahariya-Rosh Hanikra area.

The Putsker Diving Club offers boat and shore dives to various sites along the northern coast, including wrecks, reef sites, and the Rosh Hanikra area. No specific site descriptions for this particular entry have been found in public sources beyond the club's general operation area.
