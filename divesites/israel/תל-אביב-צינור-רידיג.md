## תל אביב - צינור רידיג (Tel Aviv Reading Pipe)

An artificial reef dive in Tel Aviv's northern coastal area, located near the Reading Power Station. "צינור רידיג" refers to a pipe associated with the Reading (רידינג) area — an industrial and power generation zone at the mouth of the Yarkon River.

## Overview

The "Reading Pipe" is a man-made structure (likely a decommissioned outfall or intake pipe from the Reading Power Station) that has become a recognized local dive site. Man-made structures in the Mediterranean accumulate significant marine life quickly, as hard substrate in an otherwise sandy-bottom environment provides attachment points for sponges, corals, and the fish that feed on them.

Located at approximately 32.11°N, 34.77°E, the site is just north of central Tel Aviv, accessible from the coastline near the Yarkon River mouth.
