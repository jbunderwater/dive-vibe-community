## עמוד 5 (Pole 5)

A dive site identified by numbered pole marker #5 on the northern Israeli coast near Rosh Hanikra — one of several pole-referenced sites used to navigate diving access in this dramatic cliffline area.

## Overview

On the dramatic chalk cliff coastline near Rosh Hanikra, dive sites are often identified by numbered pole markers along the shore, which help divers and boat operators locate entry points and underwater features from the water. "Pole 5" (עמוד 5) designates a dive site at the base of or offshore from the #5 marker on this stretch of coast.

The Rosh Hanikra area features diverse dive terrain including sea caves, rocky reefs, and the famous grottoes at the cape itself. Diving at numbered pole sites in this area requires IDF Navy clearance due to the proximity of the Lebanese border and requires local operator coordination.
