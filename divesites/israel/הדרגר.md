## הדרג'ר (The Dredger)

"The Dredger" (הדרג'ר) is a sunken dredging vessel located off the Jaffa coastline at approximately 32.09°N, 34.76°E. Dredgers are large working vessels used for harbor maintenance and construction, and when sunk, their substantial metal structures create excellent artificial reefs.

## Overview

The dredger wreck off Jaffa is a locally known dive site. Dredging vessels typically have a distinctive flat-bottomed hull with a large central well or bucket mechanism, creating a complex structural environment underwater. The wreck has been colonized by Mediterranean marine life. The site's coordinates place it close to the Jaffa Harbor area, where dredging operations for harbor maintenance would be historically plausible.
