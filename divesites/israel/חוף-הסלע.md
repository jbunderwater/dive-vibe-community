## חוף הסלע (Rock Beach)

"Rock Beach" (חוף הסלע) is a named beach and dive site in the Bat Yam area south of Tel Aviv. As the name suggests, the beach features rocky outcrops that extend into the sea, creating a natural reef habitat suitable for snorkeling and shore diving.

## Overview

Located at approximately 32.02°N, 34.74°E — south of Jaffa in the Bat Yam coastal area — Rock Beach is one of the more accessible natural reef dive sites along the southern Tel Aviv metropolitan shoreline. Rocky bottom begins near the waterline and extends offshore, providing substrate for Mediterranean reef life in shallow water.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Bat Yam coastal area, south of Jaffa, Israel
- **Entry Type**: Shore entry
- **Site Type**: Reef
- **Maximum Depth**: 10 meters
- **Typical Visibility**: 5-10 meters
- **Current**: Generally light; surge in rough conditions
- **Best Time**: Autumn and spring

## Marine Life

Shallow rocky Mediterranean reef species: sea bream, mullet, octopus, moray eels, scorpionfish, and invertebrates in rock crevices.

## Dive Profile

Direct shore entry from the beach. Rocky entry requires appropriate footwear. Depth is limited by the coastal topography. Suitable for beginners and snorkelers.

## Safety Considerations

Rocky entry and exit — use appropriate dive booties. Surge can be strong when swell is present. Avoid in rough weather.
