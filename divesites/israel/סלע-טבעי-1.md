## סלע טבעי 1 (Natural Rock 1)

סלע טבעי 1 — "Natural Rock 1" — is a natural rocky reef site off the Ashkelon coast in the southern Mediterranean. The name indicates a natural rock formation on the seabed, one of several numbered natural rock sites in this area.

## Overview

The Ashkelon offshore zone contains a number of natural rocky outcrops on the sandy seabed that create local reef habitat for Mediterranean species. These rock formations — tagged in OpenStreetMap as "natural rock" sites — serve as artificial oases on an otherwise sandy bottom, attracting grouper, sea bream, octopus, and other reef-associated species.
