## אי הזבובים (Fly Island)

"Fly Island" (Fly Island = אי הזבובים) is a small rocky island or islet feature in the coastal waters near Acre, northern Israel.

## Overview

Fly Island (אי הזבובים, literally "Island of the Flies") is a small coastal feature near Acre (Akko) in northern Israel. The Rosh Hanikra-Achziv Marine Reserve extends through this area, which is known for its rocky reefs, sea caves, and diverse marine life. Diving around small rocky islands and islets typically involves exploring the rocky base, swim-throughs, and overhangs.

The Rosh Hanikra-Achziv Marine Reserve was opened to visitors and divers, with authorized snorkelling and diving at designated sites. Access through local dive clubs is the standard approach for this area.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Coastal waters near Acre (Akko), northern Israel
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Reef
- **Maximum Depth**: 15 meters
- **Typical Visibility**: Typical northern Israeli Mediterranean 5–12 metres
- **Current**: Moderate
