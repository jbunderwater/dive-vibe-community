## PORT

PORT is a shore-entry dive site in the central section of Eilat's Red Sea coastline, situated in the coral beach corridor between the main resort area and the Coral Beach Nature Reserve, near the marina.

## Overview

PORT is located at approximately lat 29.534, placing it in the mid-coastline area of Eilat near the marina and resort corridor. This zone is characterized by the transition between the densely developed central hotel strip and the beginning of the protected coral beach zone. The site likely refers to a dive entry point adjacent to or near the Eilat marina facilities. The coral reef begins close to shore throughout this central Eilat corridor and the slope drops to recreational depths within a short swim. Multiple dive centers operate from nearby hotels, making this zone well-serviced for equipment rental and guided dives.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Central Eilat, marina/port area, Red Sea, Israel
- **Entry Type**: Shore entry
- **Site Type**: Coral reef
- **Maximum Depth**: 20 meters
- **Typical Visibility**: 20-35 meters
- **Current**: Very light
- **Water Temperature**: 21°C (winter) to 26°C (summer)

## Marine Life

The central Eilat reef corridor supports a wide range of Red Sea species. Anthias and chromis cloud the coral heads. Parrotfish, grouper, and Napoleon wrasse are resident reef fish. Lionfish perch in coral overhangs. The sandy patches between reef sections host garden eels and flatfish. Sea turtles are occasional visitors. Moray eels inhabit crevices throughout the coral slope.

## Dive Profile

Shore entry from a central beach or marina access point. The reef begins close to shore and the slope drops to 20+ meters. Productive diving is found between 5 and 15 meters in the coral gardens. The proximity to multiple dive centers means guided options are widely available.

## Entry and Exit

Shore entry from the central Eilat beach zone near the marina. Multiple beach access points exist along this stretch. The area has good infrastructure including hotels with dive shops, equipment rentals, and dive guides familiar with local sites.

## Tips and Recommendations

- Conveniently located near many Eilat hotels — easy day-of-arrival diving
- Multiple dive centers in the area for equipment hire and guided dives
- Good for afternoon dives after checking in to nearby accommodation
- Combine with the Coral Beach Nature Reserve entry to the south for a varied dive day

## Safety Considerations

The marina area has boat traffic. Use an SMB for ascents. Be aware of jet skis and water sports activity common in the central resort zone. Stay within marked swim zones where they exist.
