## Wreck Maapilim

A wreck associated with Ma'apilim — the illegal Jewish immigration movement to British Mandate Palestine. Located off the Haifa coast.

## Overview

"Maapilim" (מעפילים) refers to the Jewish immigrants who attempted to enter British Mandate Palestine illegally between 1934 and 1948, often on overcrowded vessels. Dozens of such ships were intercepted by the British, scuttled, or ran aground along the Israeli coast. This site, designated "Wreck Maapilim," likely refers to the wreck of one such immigrant vessel in the waters off Haifa.

Note: The Wreck Arlozorov, also in the Haifa area, is another known Ma'apilim ship wreck. It is possible that "Wreck Maapilim" is an alternative designation for the same vessel or wreck field, or a distinct, less-documented immigrant ship. No specific historical records confirming the identity of this particular vessel have been found in public sources.
