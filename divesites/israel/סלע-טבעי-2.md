## סלע טבעי 2 (Natural Rock 2)

סלע טבעי 2 — "Natural Rock 2" — is a natural rocky reef site off the Ashkelon coast in the southern Mediterranean. Together with Natural Rock 1, it forms part of a cluster of natural rock formations on the sandy seabed in this area.

## Overview

The Ashkelon offshore zone contains several natural rocky outcrops that create oasis-like reef habitat on the surrounding sandy seabed. This site lies approximately 500 metres southwest of Natural Rock 1 at 31.691°N 34.526°E, in similar water depths of an estimated 10–15 metres. It is likely dived alongside neighbouring sites Natural Rock 1 and הענבר (The Amber) in the same trip given their proximity.

The rocky substrate provides habitat for octopus, moray eels, sea bream, grouper, and other Mediterranean reef species. No site-specific published dive description has been found.
