## אתר התחמושת (Ammunition Site)

"Ammunition Site" (אתר התחמושת) is a recognized dive site off the Tel Aviv / north Jaffa coastline, named for the presence of military ammunition or ordnance on the seabed. This type of site is found along coastlines that have experienced military activity or where weapons have been disposed of at sea.

## Overview

The site is located at approximately 32.13°N, 34.76°E — in waters offshore of the Tel Aviv coastline between Herzliya and Jaffa. The "Ammunition Site" classification suggests this is a location where divers can observe ordnance (shells, munitions, or related military hardware) that has accumulated on or become embedded in the seabed. Such sites sometimes result from WWII-era naval activity, post-war ordnance dumping, or Israeli War of Independence military operations along the coast.

**Important safety note:** Do not touch, disturb, or attempt to recover any ordnance found at this site. Unexploded ordnance remains dangerous indefinitely. Observe from a safe distance only.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Offshore Tel Aviv area, central Israeli Mediterranean
- **Entry Type**: Shore entry
- **Site Type**: Coral reef
- **Maximum Depth**: Approximately 12-15 meters
- **Typical Visibility**: 5-10 meters
- **Current**: Variable
- **Best Time**: Spring and autumn

## Safety Considerations

**Never touch, move, or attempt to recover ordnance.** Unexploded shells and munitions remain potentially lethal regardless of age. Maintain distance from any identifiable ordnance. If you encounter ordnance not previously documented, report it to the Israeli Nature and Parks Authority or coast guard.
