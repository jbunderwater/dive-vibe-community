## קניון אכזיב (Achziv Canyon)

A dramatic underwater wall running parallel to the coast within Achziv National Park — one of the premier diving destinations on Israel's northern Mediterranean coast.

## Overview

Achziv Canyon (קניון אכזיב) is a long wall stretching north to south parallel to the coastline, located approximately 1 km offshore within the Achziv National Park, between Nahariya and Rosh Hanikra. The wall starts at around 10 metres and drops to 30 metres, with its face covered in sponges, soft corals, and encrusting organisms. The canyon wall is riddled with caves, fissures, and overhangs that provide habitat for a diverse range of Mediterranean marine life.

The site is too far offshore for comfortable swimming from the beach and is accessed exclusively by boat through local dive operators. The national park status provides some protection for the marine environment, and the site is considered one of northern Israel's best dives.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Achziv National Park, north of Nahariya, Israel
- **Entry Type**: Shore entry
- **Site Type**: Cave/cavern
- **Maximum Depth**: 30 metres
- **Typical Visibility**: 5–15 metres (seasonal variation; best spring-autumn)
- **Current**: Moderate; can increase along the wall
- **Best Time**: May to October

## Marine Life

The wall's caves and fissures shelter large groupers, schools of red soldierfish, Mediterranean parrotfish, and impressive Taeniura stingrays. Squids, small rays, sea turtles, and large pelagic fish are attracted to the site. Octopus are common in rocky crevices throughout the wall. The wall face is covered in colourful sponges and soft corals.

## Dive Profile

Boat dive with descent to the top of the wall at 10 metres, then following the wall face to depth. The wall profile allows divers to control their depth precisely. The cave sections and overhangs reward careful exploration. Plan a multilevel dive — spend time at depth on the wall features, then ascend for a shallower exploration of the upper wall and a safety stop.

## Tips and Recommendations

- Access through dive clubs in Nahariya (Putsker) or Shavei Zion (Indigo)
- IDF Navy clearance may be required for some zones within the national park
- A torch is useful for illuminating cave interiors and spotting hidden marine life
- The site is best dived on calm days when boat conditions allow safe entry and exit

## Safety Considerations

Moderate currents can flow along the wall. Carry an SMB for ascent. Boat traffic in the area requires caution. The wall drops to 30 metres, requiring proper dive planning for recreational divers.

## Photography

The wall covered in colourful organisms and the cave sections provide excellent macro and wide-angle opportunities. Groupers in cave mouths, stingrays on the sand at the base of the wall, and light rays from above make the Achziv wall a photogenic site.
