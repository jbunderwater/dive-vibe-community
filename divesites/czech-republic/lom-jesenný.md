## Lom Jesenný

A heart-shaped flooded limestone quarry in the Bohemian Paradise region with a VW minibus, submarine model, and small underwater cave.

## Overview

Lom Jesenný is a flooded limestone quarry located in the village of Jesenný in the Semily District, Liberec Region, within the scenic Bohemian Paradise (Český ráj) area of northern Bohemia. The quarry is roughly heart-shaped, measuring approximately 130 by 90 meters, and sits at 452 meters altitude. It reaches a maximum depth of 13.5 meters. The site is privately managed and requires advance booking through the diving base.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Jesenný, Semily District, Liberec Region (Liberecký kraj)
- **Entry Type**: Shore entry
- **Site Type**: Beach dive
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 14 meters
- **Typical Visibility**: Up to 3 meters (best in winter and spring)
- **Current**: None
- **Best Time**: Winter and spring for best visibility

## Underwater Features

The quarry contains a VW minibus, a model submarine named "Merlin," a training platform, a wreck trainer for practicing penetration skills, and a small underwater cave formation. The limestone walls and rock features add geological interest.

## Marine Life

Freshwater species typical of Bohemian quarries. Clear water periods in winter offer the best observation conditions.

## Dive Profile

Enter via stairs from the small island access point. The quarry's compact heart shape makes for easy navigation — follow the walls for a complete circuit. Visit the VW minibus, submarine model, and cave feature at various depths. The 13.5-meter maximum keeps the entire site within comfortable recreational limits.

## Access

This is a private site. Contact the diving base in advance to arrange access. Entry is from the island via purpose-built stairs down to the water.

## Tips and Recommendations

- Must call ahead to arrange access — no drop-in diving
- The small cave and wreck trainer provide good practice for overhead environment skills
- Adjust your dive computer for altitude (452 meters)
- The Bohemian Paradise area offers excellent above-water sightseeing between dives
