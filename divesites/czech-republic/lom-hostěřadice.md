## Lom Hostěřadice

A crystal-clear flooded quarry in the South Moravian wine country, managed by the Manta Znojmo diving club, packed with underwater attractions.

## Overview

Lom Hostěřadice is a flooded quarry carved from Paleozoic sediments approximately 300 million years old, located near the village of Hostěřadice in the Znojmo district of South Moravia, between Hostěřadice and Miroslav. The quarry spans 230 meters long and over 100 meters wide, reaching 12-13 meters depth. It is managed by the Manta Znojmo diving club and features crystal-clear water. The area is fenced by Lesy ČR (Czech Forests) since 2020 with a locked gate for controlled access.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Hostěřadice, Znojmo district, Jihomoravský kraj
- **Entry Type**: Shore entry
- **Site Type**: Beach dive
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 13 meters
- **Typical Visibility**: Excellent — crystal-clear water
- **Current**: None
- **Best Time**: September to June (closed July-August except by special arrangement)

## Underwater Features

The quarry is packed with deliberately placed attractions: a van, a yacht, a water sprite figure (vodník), a training platform, wine barrels (fitting for the wine-growing region), a submarine model, a boat, a diver figurine, a treasure chest, and a mirror. These provide both navigation landmarks and photography subjects.

## Marine Life

Freshwater species in the clear quarry water. Species reported by the site's managing diving club (Manta Znojmo) include carp, perch, pike, catfish, and crayfish. No site-specific species list has been independently confirmed from a second source.

## Dive Profile

A shallow, relaxed dive with excellent visibility. Enter from shore and explore the scattered attractions across the quarry floor. The maximum depth of 12-13 meters keeps the entire site within recreational limits. The clear water and numerous points of interest make this ideal for photography.

## Access and Fees

Access requires borrowing a key from the BENALE gas station in Miroslav upon showing a valid dive license and registration. The fee is 100 CZK per diver. The site is closed during July and August; diving outside these months is only by special arrangement with the Manta Znojmo diving club.

## Tips and Recommendations

- Bring an underwater camera — the clear water and numerous attractions offer great photo opportunities
- Plan your visit outside July-August when the site is closed
- Pick up the gate key at the BENALE gas station in Miroslav before heading to the quarry
