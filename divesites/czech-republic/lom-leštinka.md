## Lom Leštinka

One of the Czech Republic's most popular dive quarries, with a terraced underwater profile, a caisson with breathable air, and full dive center facilities.

## Overview

Lom Leštinka is a flooded granite quarry near Skuteč in the Pardubický region, sitting at 430 meters altitude. It is one of the most frequently visited dive sites in the country, with two diving bases on site providing cylinder fills, equipment rental, refreshments, and accommodation. The quarry covers approximately 1 hectare and features a terraced bottom profile reaching a maximum depth of 26 meters. An old mining station at 12 meters preserves tools, carts, and building remnants from the quarry's active years.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Near Skuteč, Pardubický kraj (altitude 430m)
- **Entry Type**: Shore entry
- **Site Type**: Wall dive
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 26 meters
- **Typical Visibility**: 3-8 meters (season-dependent)
- **Current**: None
- **Best Time**: Spring and autumn; winter for ice diving

## Underwater Features

The highlight is a caisson at 20 meters depth — approximately 3,500 liters with breathable air regularly refreshed from the surface. Training platforms sit at 3, 6, 8, and 12 meters. At 12 meters, the remains of the old mining station include tools, mine carts, and building structures. A sunken museum provides additional exploration. Submerged forests and quarry walls add to the atmosphere.

## Marine Life

Rich freshwater life: pike, large koi carp, bream, sturgeon, catfish, mussels, and crayfish. The quarry's established ecosystem supports more diversity than most Czech dive sites.

## Dive Profile

Multiple depth levels allow progression from beginner to advanced dives. Start at the shallow training platforms (3-8 meters), descend to the mining station at 12 meters, and explore deeper to the caisson at 20 meters. The terraced profile naturally supports multi-level ascent planning. The deepest areas reach 26 meters.

## Tips and Recommendations

- Two on-site dive bases provide everything needed — fills, rental, food, and beds
- The caisson at 20 meters is a unique experience — hover inside with breathable air
- Adjust your dive computer for altitude diving (430 meters elevation)
- Combine with the nearby Leštinka - Kaňon quarry for a varied dive day
- Excellent access by car with parking at the water's edge
