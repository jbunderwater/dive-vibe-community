## Hranická Propast

The deepest flooded pit cave on Earth — a 519-meter karst abyss in eastern Moravia whose true bottom remains unknown.

## Overview

Hranická Propast (Hranice Abyss) is located within the Hůrka u Hranic National Nature Reserve on the right bank of the Bečva River, near the town of Hranice in the Olomoucký region. A 69.5-meter dry shaft leads down to the water surface, below which the cave extends at least 450 meters underwater — confirmed in 2022 by the UX1-Neo AUV operated by UNEXMIN Georobotics. The total confirmed depth from the surface is 519.5 meters (69.5 m dry + 450 m underwater), making it the deepest flooded pit cave in the world. The true bottom remains unknown.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Hůrka u Hranic Nature Reserve, near Hranice, Olomoucký kraj
- **Entry Type**: Shore entry
- **Site Type**: Cave/cavern
- **Difficulty Level**: Advanced
- **Maximum Depth**: 450 meters
- **Current**: None
- **Water Temperature**: Thermal mineral water, warmer than surrounding quarries

## Notable Explorations

Polish explorer Krzysztof Starnawski set multiple depth records over successive expeditions: reaching 181 m in 2000, 217 m in 2012, and 265 m in 2015 — all on closed-circuit rebreather. In 2016, a GRALmarine ROV reached 404 m. In August 2022, the UX1-Neo AUV operated by UNEXMIN Georobotics descended to 450 m underwater without reaching the bottom. Access to the cave for diving is restricted to members of the Hranický Karst Diving Club; open-circuit equipment is prohibited due to bubble damage to cave walls. The cave's geological formation involves upwelling waters, though the precise mechanism remains under scientific study.

## Safety Considerations

This is not a recreational dive site. Access is restricted within the national nature reserve and requires special permits. Only professional cave divers with extreme-depth experience and specialized equipment should attempt any diving here. The site has been the focus of scientific expeditions, not recreational visits.

## Tips and Recommendations

- This site is included for completeness as a world-record natural feature in the Czech Republic
- Recreational divers can visit the nature reserve above ground and view the dry shaft entrance
- The nearby Lom Opatovice u Hranic offers accessible quarry diving in the same region
