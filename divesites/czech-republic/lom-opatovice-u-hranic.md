## Lom Opatovice u Hranic

A challenging flooded greywacke quarry near Hranice known for poor visibility, cold bottom temperatures, and submerged excavators — strictly for experienced divers.

## Overview

Lom Opatovice is a flooded stone quarry (droba/greywacke sandstone) located approximately 6 km from Hranice along road 438 in the Olomoucký region. Mining ended in 1990 and the quarry filled naturally, completing in 1997 partly with flood water. The quarry is irregularly oval, roughly 150 by 140 meters, with a maximum depth of approximately 37 meters including about 1 meter of bottom sediment washed in from surrounding agricultural fields.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Opatovice, near Hranice, Olomoucký kraj
- **Entry Type**: Shore entry
- **Site Type**: Beach dive
- **Difficulty Level**: Advanced
- **Maximum Depth**: 37 meters
- **Water Temperature**: 3-5°C at the bottom year-round
- **Typical Visibility**: Poor and variable — milky/opaque band between 3-15 meters; surface 2-4 meters; bottom 0-4 meters
- **Current**: None

## Underwater Features

Two tracked excavators remain on the quarry floor, along with a small building containing pumps and numerous submerged trees. The bottom sediment is fine-grained from agricultural runoff.

## Marine Life

Limited freshwater life due to poor visibility and cold temperatures.

## Dive Profile

Descend from shore along a sloping entry. Be prepared for a milky thermocline layer between 3-15 meters where visibility drops significantly. Below this band, conditions may improve slightly but bottom visibility can range from zero to 4 meters. Navigation is primarily by compass.

## Safety Considerations

This site is explicitly not suitable for beginners. Required equipment includes two independent regulators, quality primary and backup lights, a dry suit, and a functional knife. Divers must have good trim control, comfort with zero visibility, compass navigation skills, and proper frog-kick technique. Hazards include buoyancy issues from temperature changes, regulator freezing in the 3-5°C bottom water, and entanglement risk from submerged tree branches.

## Tips and Recommendations

- Carry redundant everything — lights, regulators, and gas supply
- Practice zero-visibility navigation before attempting this site
- A line reel is essential for maintaining orientation
