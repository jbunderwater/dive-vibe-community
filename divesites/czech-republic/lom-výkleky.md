## Lom Výkleky

A shallow, accessible quarry near Olomouc favored by recreational divers for its good facilities, attractive rock formations, and seasonal freshwater jellyfish sightings.

## Overview

Lom Výkleky is a flooded stone quarry located approximately 25 km from Olomouc in the Olomoucký region. The quarry measures approximately 100 by 340 meters with a maximum depth of around 9 meters and a rocky bottom averaging 6 meters. It is one of the most popular weekend dive destinations in Moravia thanks to easy access, good facilities, and a reliable underwater environment. The site is particularly notable for seasonal sightings of freshwater jellyfish — an unusual and photogenic attraction.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Near Přerov, approximately 25 km from Olomouc, Olomoucký kraj
- **Entry Type**: Shore entry
- **Site Type**: Beach dive
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 9 meters
- **Typical Visibility**: 3-5 meters, consistent
- **Current**: None
- **Best Time**: Year-round; jellyfish appear in late summer

## Underwater Features

Sunken attractions include a Škoda 1203 van wreck, a caisson, a bicycle, a caravan, and a bus stop sign. The rocky bottom and quarry walls provide natural interest between the placed objects.

## Marine Life

Freshwater fish and invertebrates. The site is notable for seasonal freshwater jellyfish (Craspedacusta sowerbii) — tiny, translucent creatures that appear in late summer when water temperatures are warm enough.

## Dive Profile

A straightforward shallow dive. Enter from shore and explore the scattered attractions across the quarry floor. The consistent depth of 6-9 meters keeps the entire site within easy recreational limits. Navigate between the Škoda wreck, caisson, and other objects for a leisurely exploration.

## Tips and Recommendations

- Visit in late summer for a chance to see the rare freshwater jellyfish
- Good facilities on site make this ideal for a relaxed weekend dive day
- The shallow depth and calm conditions make this excellent for photography practice
- A dry suit is recommended outside of summer months
