## Isola dei Cavoli

Isola dei Cavoli is a reef dive site in Sardinia, Europe.

## Overview

Isola dei Cavoli is a small granite island within the Capo Carbonara Marine Protected Area near Villasimius in southeastern Sardinia, where massive boulders create deep fissures, cavities, and eroded formations descending to over 40 metres. Divers visit the iconic Madonna del Naufrago statue at shallow depth, explore the ship-shaped granite monolith of La Nave Romana, and encounter red gorgonians at the Sandbank of Mezzo. The crystalline waters shelter schools of barracuda, mullet, lobsters, dentex, amberjack, sea bream, salps, and damselfish among the granite formations. The Madonna del Naufrago statue sits at 10-12 metres depth and was placed here in 1979.
