## MuMart (museo sommerso)

MuMart (museo sommerso) is a reef dive site in Sardinia, Europe.

## Overview

MuMart (Maritime Museum of Art) is Sardinia's underwater art museum, inaugurated July 26, 2013. The museum consists of contemporary sculptures by 14 artists anchored to the sandy seabed at Golfo Aranci between 4 and 7 metres depth, near the Terza Spiaggia lagoon. The works are accessible by guided dive with local dive centres, by snorkelling, or by the glass-bottom submarine Mizar I (operating in summer from Golfo Aranci harbour). The shallow depth and calm conditions make it one of the most accessible dive experiences in northeastern Sardinia.
