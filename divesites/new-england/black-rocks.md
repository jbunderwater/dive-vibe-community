## Overview

Black Rocks is a tidal rock cluster at lat 42.821, lon -70.827 (Hampton/Seabrook NH coastal zone, Plum Island/Merrimack River approach area). OSM classifies it as `seamark:obstruction:category=foul_ground` and `seamark:rock:water_level=covers` (tidal), designated as a named geographic locality (`place=locality`). It sits within the Hampton/Seabrook cluster of named navigational hazards including Lunts Rock, Badgers Rock, Half Tide Rocks, and Sow and Pigs — all within approximately 1 km of each other in the outer approaches to Plum Island Sound.
