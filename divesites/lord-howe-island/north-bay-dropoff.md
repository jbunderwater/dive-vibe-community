## North Bay Dropoff

## Overview

North Bay is primarily known as a snorkelling and freediving location centred on the wreck of the MV Favourite (an American tuna vessel that sank in 1965 after dragging anchor at the entrance to North Passage). The wreck is extremely shallow — much of it nearly dries at low water — and is technically a snorkel site.
