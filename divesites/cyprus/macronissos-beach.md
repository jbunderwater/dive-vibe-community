# Macronissos Beach

Golden sand beaches with shallow rocky dives in consistently clear water — perfect for introductory experiences.

## Overview

Macronissos Beach is located 5 km west of central Ayia Napa, featuring three distinct beaches with golden sand. Shallow dives through rocky underwater topography with consistently clear water and excellent visibility. Used for introductory and Discover Scuba experiences. No strong currents.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Ayia Napa, east Cyprus
- **Entry Type**: Shore entry
- **Site Type**: Coral reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Beginner
- **Maximum Depth**: 10 meters
- **Typical Visibility**: 15–30 meters
- **Current**: Light to moderate
- **Best Time**: March to November

## Marine Life

Colourful reef fish and sea turtles have been reported in the area.

## Tips and Recommendations

- Check conditions with local operators before diving
- Carry a torch for cave and overhang exploration
- Dive within your certification limits
