# Macronissos Beach

Three sandy bays with shallow rocky diving 5 km west of Ayia Napa — calm and clear, used for introductory dive experiences.

## Overview

Macronissos Beach is located approximately 5 km west of central Ayia Napa, on the east coast of Cyprus. Three distinct bays extend in different directions from a 350-metre beach, with fine golden sand and consistently clear water. Maximum depth is approximately 10 metres. The site is calm with no significant current and is used for introductory and Discover Scuba experiences by Ayia Napa dive operators. Nearby is the Makronissos archaeological site with ancient tombs.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Ayia Napa, east Cyprus (5 km west of town centre)
- **Entry Type**: Shore entry
- **Site Type**: Reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Beginner
- **Maximum Depth**: 10 meters
- **Typical Visibility**: 12–15 meters
- **Current**: Light
- **Best Time**: March to November

## Marine Life

No site-specific marine life reports found. Regional Mediterranean reef species apply.

## Tips and Recommendations

- Three distinct bays offering variety in a single visit
- Very calm conditions — suitable for introductory experiences
- Nearby Makronissos archaeological site adds interest for surface intervals
