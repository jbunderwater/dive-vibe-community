# Cape Grecko Caves

Over a dozen caves including The Cathedral, Love Bridge, and the Blue Room — all naturally illuminated in Cape Greco National Park.

## Overview

Cape Greco Caves within the National Park features over a dozen caves, tunnels, and swim-throughs at 14–15 metres depth. Named features include The Cathedral (a larger cavern with dramatic light shafts), Love Bridge Cave (a swim-through beneath a natural rock arch), and the Blue Room (known for its ethereal blue light). The lava rock formations include one cave interior shaped like the island of Cyprus when viewed from inside. Natural light illuminates all cave passages.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Cape Greco National Park, east Cyprus
- **Entry Type**: Shore entry
- **Site Type**: Cave/cavern
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 15 meters
- **Typical Visibility**: 25–40 meters
- **Current**: Light
- **Best Time**: March to November

## Marine Life

Bearded fire worms, ornate wrasse, white bream, groupers, octopus, moray eels, sea turtles, nudibranchs, sponges, fairy basslets, and starburst anemones.

## Tips and Recommendations

- Over a dozen caves and swim-throughs — varied exploration
- The Cathedral, Love Bridge, and Blue Room are the highlight features
- One cave interior is shaped like the island of Cyprus
- All passages naturally illuminated — safe for Open Water divers
- Shore entry with short rocky walk
