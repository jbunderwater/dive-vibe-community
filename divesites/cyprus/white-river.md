# White River

A 30-metre-long swim-through shaped by ancient river erosion — dramatic geological formations unique to Paphos.

## Overview

White River features a prominent 30-metre-long swim-through, plus gullies, overhangs, and curved rocks shaped by ancient river erosion. Natural light illuminates the passage, making it excellent for underwater photography. The geological formations created by the eroded river bed give this site its distinctive character.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Paphos west coast, Cyprus
- **Entry Type**: Shore entry
- **Site Type**: Cave/cavern
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 12 meters
- **Typical Visibility**: 15–30 meters
- **Current**: Light to moderate
- **Best Time**: March to November

## Marine Life

Various species inhabiting the crevices and overhangs of the eroded rock formations.

## Tips and Recommendations

- Check conditions with local operators before diving
- Carry a torch for cave and overhang exploration
- Dive within your certification limits
