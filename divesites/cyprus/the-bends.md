# The Bends

A reef site in the Paphos west coast dive corridor between Sea Caves and Amphitheatre.

## Overview

The Bends is located in the Paphos west coast diving corridor between Sea Caves and Amphitheatre, part of the Coral Bay dive site cluster. The site features typical Paphos coast topography with rocky reef formations.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Paphos west coast, Cyprus
- **Entry Type**: Shore entry
- **Site Type**: Coral reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 20 meters
- **Typical Visibility**: 15–30 meters
- **Current**: Light to moderate
- **Best Time**: March to November

## Marine Life

Mediterranean reef species typical of the Paphos coast.

## Tips and Recommendations

- Check conditions with local operators before diving
- Carry a torch for cave and overhang exploration
- Dive within your certification limits
