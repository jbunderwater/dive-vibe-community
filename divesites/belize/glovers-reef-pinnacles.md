## Glover's Reef Pinnacles

Glover's Reef Pinnacles is one of the signature dive sites at Glover's Reef Atoll, the southernmost coral atoll in Belize, located approximately 45 kilometres (28 miles) off Belize's southern coast.

## Overview

Glover's Reef Atoll is an UNESCO World Heritage Site (designated 1996) measuring 32 kilometres (20 miles) long and 12 kilometres (7.5 miles) wide, with an interior lagoon containing approximately 850 patch reefs and coral pinnacles. The Pinnacles site features coral structures that rise from about 30 metres (100 feet) depth to within about 8 metres (25 feet) of the surface, creating dramatic vertical terrain covered in sponges, sea fans (gorgonians), and encrusting corals.

Belize Dive Haven Resort's site description of The Pinnacles confirms large sponge formations and gorgonian sea fans on each pinnacle structure. Sand channels running between the pinnacles are specifically documented as habitat for snappers, Caribbean reef sharks, and mackerel. The Aquarium, a nearby Glover's Reef site, is noted for schools of creole wrasse and yellowtail snappers; Long Caye Wall is described as a vertical wall face dropping at least 900 metres beginning just below 10 metres (35 feet) depth.

Glover's Reef hosts one of only two known remaining viable Nassau grouper spawning aggregations in the western Caribbean (per Wikipedia's Glover's Reef article citing Wildlife Conservation Society research). This aggregation occurs at the northeastern end of the atoll and is subject to seasonal fishing closure (December–February).

Access to Glover's Reef requires a boat ride of approximately 1.5–2 hours from Placencia or Hopkins. All diving at the atoll is done from boat; the marine reserve requires a permit. Intermediate-level certification is the practical minimum for the depth range at the Pinnacles site.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Glover's Reef Marine Reserve, ~45 km off southern Belize (Stann Creek / Toledo districts)
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Pinnacle/seamount
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 30 meters
