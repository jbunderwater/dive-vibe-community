## Witconcrete Wreck

Witconcrete Wreck is one of the largest wreck dive sites in the Caribbean, an intentionally sunk concrete-hulled vessel resting in the Soldier Caye Conservation Zone of the Turneffe Atoll Marine Reserve, approximately 1.2 kilometres (0.75 miles) northeast of Blackbird Caye.

## Overview

The Witconcrete (also written "The Wit") is a concrete-hulled barge measuring approximately 114 metres (375 feet) in length, 17 metres (56 feet) wide, and 12 metres (38 feet) in height. It was originally constructed between 1942 and 1944 as a wartime supply vessel operating along the U.S. Gulf Coast and Eastern Seaboard. After the war, the vessel was eventually repurposed for molasses storage by Belize Sugar Industries Ltd., which donated it to the Turneffe Atoll Sustainability Association in May 2021.

The vessel was deliberately submerged on 10 December 2021. It rests on its starboard side with the bow pointing roughly north in a sandy area at approximately 18 metres (60 feet) depth, on a gentle slope designed to allow both diving and snorkeling access. Reef Smart Guides records the GPS position as 17° 20.433'N, 88° 11.533'W.

Sixteen internal chambers were made accessible for divers prior to sinking. As of the deployment date, the wreck structure was complete but marine colonization was in early stages; operators and the Caribbean Lifestyle article on the sinking note that the vessel is expected to develop into a substantial artificial reef over time. Penetration of internal compartments is available for certified, experienced divers only.

**Note on marine life:** Published accounts at the time of sinking describe expected future colonizers (grouper, snapper, nurse sharks, eagle rays, sea turtles) rather than confirmed post-sinking surveys. The wreck was sunk in December 2021; site-specific post-sinking marine life surveys were not located in sources consulted.

A confirmed sister ship — Wit Concrete II — was previously sunk in the U.S. Virgin Islands in 1995. This is documented in the Caribbean Lifestyle sinking account.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Soldier Caye Conservation Zone, Turneffe Atoll Marine Reserve (~0.75 miles NE of Blackbird Caye)
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Wreck dive
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 18 meters
- **Wreck Sunk**: 10 December 2021 (intentional deployment)
