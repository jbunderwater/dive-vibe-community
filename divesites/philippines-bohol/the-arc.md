# The Arc

A reef and mixed-terrain dive site off Panglao Island near the former Bohol Beach Club, in the Libaong area. The site area includes sandy slopes, coral pinnacles, and accessible diving for all levels.

## Overview

The Arc is located off Panglao Island in the Libaong area, near the former Bohol Beach Club resort. Its coordinates sit very close to those of the nearby Arco Point site; it may represent the broader BBC dive area rather than a distinct named site. The terrain in this section of Panglao's coast includes sandy slopes with coral pinnacles and mixed reef formations at depths ranging to approximately 25 meters.

The site is suitable for beginner and intermediate divers. Conditions are generally calm with mild currents. Macro life is the primary draw in this area, consistent with other nearby Panglao reef sites.

**Note:** No dive operator lists "The Arc" as a distinct named site separate from Arco Point or the BBC dive area. This entry could not be independently validated under this name from three separate sources; its relationship to the adjacent Arco Point entry (9.555, 123.775) should be confirmed with local operators.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Libaong area, Panglao Island, Bohol, Philippines (near former Bohol Beach Club)
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Coral reef and sandy slope
- **Difficulty Level**: Beginner
- **Maximum Depth**: 25 meters
- **Typical Visibility**: 10-25 meters
- **Water Temperature**: 26-30 degrees C
- **Current**: Generally mild
- **Best Time**: Year-round

## Marine Life

The BBC area supports macro life typical of Panglao: frogfish varieties, nudibranchs, banded sea kraits, moray eels, and sun coral. Reef fish include small groupers, triggerfish, wrasses, and anthias. Sandy patches adjacent to reef host cryptic macro subjects.

## Dive Profile

Descend along the sloping reef from the shallows to approximately 25 meters. Mixed terrain of coral pinnacles and sandy patches offers a variety of micro-habitats. The gradual slope makes depth management straightforward.

## Entry and Exit

Access by dive boat from Alona Beach area. Entry by giant stride.

## Tips and Recommendations

- A good site for macro photography
- Can be combined with nearby Arco Point for a multi-dive morning
- Confirm with your dive operator whether "The Arc" is a distinct site or overlaps with BBC/Arco Point

## Safety Considerations

Mild conditions make this one of the more accessible Panglao sites. Standard dive safety practices apply.
