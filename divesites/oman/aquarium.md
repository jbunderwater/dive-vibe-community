## Aquarium

Aquarium is a reef dive site near Mirbat in Oman's Dhofar region.

## Overview

Aquarium is a shallow coral garden dive site near Mirbat in southern Oman, named for the density and variety of marine life on its reef. Bluewater Dive Travel describes a site by this name in the Hallaniyat Islands area featuring a small protected bay with huge colourful table corals spreading over a large area, with hundreds of shrimp gobies throughout the dive and some of the largest moray eels in the region in deeper crevices. The PADI Pros blog lists Aquarium among the Mirbat area dive sites as a shallow coral garden environment hosting diverse species from small crustaceans to larger reef fish. The site is accessible by boat from Salalah-based operators during the October to May dive season, outside the khareef monsoon months.

*Note: "Aquarium" is a common name for sheltered coral garden sites. The site at these coordinates (16.95°N, 54.80°E) is clearly near Mirbat; one source (Bluewater Dive Travel) places an "Aquarium" at the Hallaniyat Islands, which are separate. Local diver reports from the Mirbat area confirm an "Aquarium" site near Mirbat with shallow coral gardens.*
