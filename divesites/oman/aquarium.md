## Aquarium

Aquarium is a reef dive site in the Mirbat area of Oman's Dhofar region.

## Overview

Aquarium is a coral reef site in the Mirbat area, approximately 70 kilometres from Salalah in Oman's Dhofar region. The site takes its name from the exceptional density of fish life — large table corals spread across a wide shallow reef, and the abundance of small fish and critters moving through the coral heads gives the site an aquarium-like quality. Sources describe hundreds of shrimp gobies distributed across the site, with morays, crabs, and shrimps occupying the reef's crevices, and rays, turtles, and barracudas patrolling the surrounding water.

Aquarium is shore-accessible and characterised by shallow depth and abundant sunlight that favours coral growth. The site is operated seasonally from October to May.

**Note:** OSM originally classified this site as a wall dive. Multiple sources from Salalah operators describe it as a coral reef/garden site rather than a vertical wall. Site type has been corrected to reef.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Mirbat area, Dhofar region, Oman (approx. 70km from Salalah)
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 24 meters
- **Season**: October to May (closed during khareef monsoon)
