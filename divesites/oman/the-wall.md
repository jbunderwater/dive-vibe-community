## The Wall

The Wall is a wall dive site in the Mirbat area of Oman's Dhofar region.

## Overview

The Wall is a vertical reef structure in the Mirbat area, approximately 70 kilometres from Salalah in Oman's Dhofar region. The site descends along a rocky cliff face to around 24 metres and is part of the cluster of dive sites concentrated along this stretch of the Dhofar coastline. Like other Mirbat sites, it reflects the Indo-Pacific character typical of southern Oman's marine life, which distinguishes this region from the Gulf of Oman diving found further north near Musandam.

The site is accessible by boat and operated seasonally from October to May, outside the annual khareef (monsoon) season that closes the Dhofar coast to diving each summer.
