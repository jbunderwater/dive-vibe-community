## The Wall

The Wall is a wall dive site near Mirbat in southern Oman's Dhofar region, one of the deepest sites in the Salalah area.

## Overview

The Wall is a vertical reef structure along the Mirbat coastline in Oman's Dhofar region, descending to around 24 metres. The site sits near Flamingo Beach and is operated by ABT Divers out of Salalah. The Indo-Pacific character of the marine life at Mirbat — unusual for the Arabian Peninsula — extends to The Wall, where batfish, shoaling snappers, and moray eels are commonly reported along the face. Turtles, stingrays, and cuttlefish are regular encounters. Available October to May before the annual khareef monsoon season.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Mirbat coast, Dhofar, southern Oman
- **Entry Type**: Shore entry
- **Site Type**: Wall dive
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 24 meters
- **Season**: October to May (khareef monsoon closes the coast June–September)

## Marine Life

Reported at The Wall: batfish, shoaling snappers, moray eels, turtles, stingrays, and cuttlefish. The Indo-Pacific reef community is distinctive for the Arabian Peninsula.
