## Snoopy Island Reef

Snoopy Island Reef is an artificial reef/wreck dive site off Al Aqah, Fujairah, UAE.

## Overview

**Geographic note:** Despite being listed under the Oman destination in OSM, this site's coordinates (25.49°N, 56.36°E) place it at Al Aqah in Fujairah, United Arab Emirates — not in Oman. The site is classified as a protected area in OSM data under Oman in error.

At 24 metres depth, this site corresponds to the dive site commonly called **Snoopy Deep Reef** (also known as "New Reef"), located approximately 500 metres east of the well-known shallow Snoopy Island. Snoopy Deep Reef is an artificial reef built around a purposely-sunk dive boat and trailer, now encrusted in hard and soft corals. The wreck provides structure for reef fish including angelfish, boxfish, snappers, wrasse, and moray eels.

This site should not be confused with the shallow Snoopy Island itself (5 to 8 metres depth, beginner-friendly), which is a separate, shallower dive/snorkel site accessible from shore or by short boat ride. The 24-metre depth of this site requires at least Advanced Open Water certification.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Al Aqah, Fujairah, UAE (listed under Oman in OSM — geographic error)
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Wreck dive
- **Difficulty Level**: Advanced
- **Maximum Depth**: 24 meters
- **Best Season**: Year-round (UAE east coast)
