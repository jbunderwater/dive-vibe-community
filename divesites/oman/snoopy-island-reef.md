## Snoopy Island Reef

Snoopy Island Reef is a beginner-friendly shallow reef surrounding a small rocky island off Al Aqah beach in Fujairah, UAE.

## Overview

Snoopy Island sits off the Al Aqah beachfront in Fujairah, UAE, named for the island's silhouette resembling the cartoon beagle lying on his back when viewed from shore. The surrounding reef is a protected marine area where fishing is prohibited, with depths mostly between 5 and 15 metres. Both shore and boat access are possible.

Confirmed marine life includes green and hawksbill turtles, moray eels, cuttlefish, trumpet fish, parrotfish, angelfish, butterflyfish, and occasional reef sharks in the surrounding waters. The site is suitable for beginners and snorkelers due to its shallow, calm, clear waters. Note: "Snoopy Deep Reef," a separate site approximately 500m east at depths reaching 24m, is designated for Advanced Open Water divers.
