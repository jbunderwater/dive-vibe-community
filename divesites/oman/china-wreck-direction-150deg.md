## China Wreck (direction 150deg)

China Wreck is a wreck dive site in the Mirbat area of Oman's Dhofar region.

## Overview

The China Wreck is a wreck dive site lying along the Mirbat coastline in Oman's Dhofar region, accessible from Salalah operators. The directional label "150deg" in the site name is a navigational bearing from shore used to locate the wreck. The wreck lies at depths of up to 25 metres and is reached by boat, operated seasonally from October to May.

**Vessel identity:** The wreck is believed by local researchers to be the SS Kiva (also spelled Kheva), a passenger vessel that departed Bombay bound for Mecca in 1893 with approximately 979 pilgrims aboard. A fire broke out in the cargo hold; the crew steered the stricken vessel toward Mirbat port, where local residents helped evacuate passengers before the ship burned through the night and sank approximately 300 metres from Mirbat beach. Reporting in the Oman Observer (2021) and Muscat Daily (2021) described Oman's Ministry of Heritage and Tourism conducting a formal documentation survey of a wreck off Mirbat — a vessel approximately 128 years old at the time — identified as the Kiva. Dive operators in the region have subsequently linked this surveyed wreck to the China Wreck site.

**Conflict note:** One Salalah operator describes the site only as "the remains of a former steam-powered vessel scattered over approximately 80 metres in a north-south direction" without naming the Kiva. The name "China Wreck" may alternatively reflect porcelain or china cargo found among the debris. The Kiva identification is plausible and supported by two independent news sources covering the Ministry survey, but the link between the surveyed wreck and this specific dive site has not been confirmed by a primary maritime database (Lloyd's Register, Wreck Site, etc.). Treat the Kiva identity as probable but not fully verified.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Mirbat area, Dhofar region, Oman (approx. 70km from Salalah)
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Wreck dive
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 25 meters
- **Season**: October to May (closed during khareef monsoon)
- **Vessel Identity**: Probable SS Kiva (1893) — identified in 2021 Oman Ministry heritage survey; not yet confirmed by Lloyd's Register or primary maritime database
