## Flamingo Beach: New Entry

Flamingo Beach: New Entry is a reef dive entry point along the Mirbat coastline in Oman's Dhofar region.

## Overview

Flamingo Beach is a coastal area near Mirbat, south of Salalah, where several access routes give access to the shallow reef system. The Mirbat coast features Indo-Pacific marine life characteristic of the Dhofar region, with table corals and brain coral formations at 5 to 15 metres supporting clownfish, moray eels, octopuses, and nudibranch species. Stingrays, cuttlefish, and turtles are encountered throughout the area. The sheltered conditions make the Flamingo Beach area accessible to divers of all skill levels. This "New Entry" is one of multiple access points around the bay; no source describes it as a separately characterised dive distinct from the wider Flamingo Beach reef. The site is available from October to May.
