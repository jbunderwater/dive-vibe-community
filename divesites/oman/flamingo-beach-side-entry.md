## Flamingo Beach: Side Entry

Flamingo Beach: Side Entry is a reef dive entry point along the Mirbat coastline in Oman's Dhofar region.

## Overview

Flamingo Beach is a coastal area near Mirbat, south of Salalah, where several shore and near-shore entry points give access to the shallow reef system. The Mirbat coast features Indo-Pacific marine life characteristic of the Dhofar region, with table corals and brain coral formations at 5 to 15 metres supporting clownfish, moray eels, octopuses, and nudibranch species. Turtles, stingrays, and cuttlefish are encountered throughout the area. The sheltered conditions make the Flamingo Beach area accessible to divers of all skill levels. This specific "Side Entry" variant is one of several access routes around the bay. The site is available from October to May once the monsoon season has passed.
