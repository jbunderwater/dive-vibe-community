## Flamingo Beach: Beach Entry

Flamingo Beach: Beach Entry is a shore reef dive site near Mirbat in Oman's Dhofar region.

## Overview

Flamingo Beach is a dive area along the Mirbat coastline of southern Oman's Dhofar region, approximately 70 kilometres east of Salalah. This beach entry point provides direct wade-in access to the shallow reef. The PADI Pros blog confirms shore diving in the Mirbat area at depths to 15 metres, with the coastal reef hosting Indo-Pacific marine life including nudibranchs, clownfish, octopuses, moray eels, and rays. Beautiful Salalah describes the Mirbat dive sites as reaching diving from the shore with no boat required. The Regaldive guide notes the area sites are suitable for all certification levels. The site is accessible during the October to May dive season, outside the annual khareef monsoon.
