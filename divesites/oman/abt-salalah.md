## Overview

ABT Salalah is a wreck dive site in the Dhofar region of southern Oman. The site name derives from ABT Divers, the local dive operator that has been running scuba diving in Salalah since at least 2010 — the site is not named after a vessel. The identity of the vessel at this site is not confirmed by available sources; vessel name, tonnage, and sinking date are unknown.

The site's coordinates place it closer to Salalah city than the Mirbat cluster of wreck sites (China Wreck, Eagle Bay, Aquarium), approximately 60 km west of Mirbat — suggesting it is a geographically distinct location. Local operator sources indicate the wreck lies at depths exceeding 30 metres, making this an Advanced-level dive. It is reached by boat and operates seasonally from October to May, outside the khareef monsoon period.
