## Rif Sint Marie

Rif Sint Marie is the house reef of Coral Estates Luxury Resort on Curaçao's west coast — a fringing reef accessible from the resort pier, dropping to 40 metres with a second reef edge at 60 metres, with confirmed parrotfish, pufferfish, trumpetfish, barracudas, and sea turtles.

## Overview

Rif Sint Marie serves as the house reef for Coral Divers Curaçao, operating out of Coral Estates Luxury Resort in Sint Willibrordus on the west coast. Day-trippers can access the reef after checking in with Coral Divers. The resort provides full amenities including a beach club, restaurant, spa, and infinity pool.

The reef begins just off the resort beach and drops to 40 metres. A second reef edge begins at 60 metres depth for advanced divers. Visibility often exceeds 30 metres. The protected location provides calm conditions throughout the year.

## Site Information

- **Location**: West coast, Coral Estates Luxury Resort, Sint Willibrordus
- **Entry Type**: Shore
- **Site Type**: Reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Beginner
- **Maximum Depth**: 25 meters
- **Typical Visibility**: 30+ metres
- **Current**: Usually minimal to none

## Marine Life

Confirmed species at this site: parrotfish, pufferfish (pufferfishes), trumpetfish, barracudas, sea turtles. The reef supports typical Caribbean reef fish diversity across its depth range.

## Dive Profile

Entry via the resort pier next to Coral Divers. The reef begins immediately offshore. The fringing reef structure extends from the beach to 40 metres depth. A second reef edge at 60 metres is accessible for advanced/technical divers. Multiple dive profiles available across the depth range.

## Entry and Exit

Shore entry from the resort beach via the Coral Divers pier. Non-resort guests should check in with Coral Divers before diving. Equipment rental, guided dives, and PADI training are available on-site.

## Tips and Recommendations

- Check in with Coral Divers before entering the water — they manage access for day visitors
- The resort amenities (restaurant, infinity pool) make this a comfortable full-day diving destination
- The site is best combined with a meal or beach time at Coral Estates
- Visibility regularly exceeds 30 metres — bring a camera

## Safety Considerations

A surface marker buoy is required when ascending. Boat traffic from Coral Divers operations is present. The resort setting provides readily available emergency support if needed.
