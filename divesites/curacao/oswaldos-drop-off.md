## Oswaldo's Drop Off

Oswaldo's Drop Off is the house reef wall dive of Ocean Encounters at Sunscape Curaçao Resort — a spectacular drop-off with best marine life at 6–12 metres, accessible exclusively to resort guests via a pier entry, or from the adjacent Pierbaai public beach.

## Overview

Oswaldo's Drop Off is located at the Sunscape Curaçao Resort, Spa and Casino on Curaçao's southeast coast. It is the house reef of Ocean Encounters, Curaçao's largest dive operator. Shore diving access is restricted to Sunscape resort guests. Non-guests can access the same reef from Pierbaai — the local beach adjacent to the resort — which reaches the same reef system from the other end.

The wall dive features a drop-off with best marine life concentrated at 20–40 feet (6–12 metres) depth. Reports note it can keep going well past 33 metres (109 feet). Unexpected strong current can occur, making it unsuitable for inexperienced divers in those conditions. The site is adjacent to the Car Pile artificial reef and the Marie Pampoen reef system.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Southeast coast, Sunscape Curaçao Resort (resort guests); or from Pierbaai public beach (non-guests)
- **Entry Type**: Shore
- **Site Type**: Wall dive
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 36 meters
- **Best Diving Depth**: 6–12 metres
- **Typical Visibility**: 20–30 metres
- **Current**: Usually minimal; unexpected strong current possible

## Marine Life

The site hosts restored coral colonies along the wall face. Generic Caribbean reef fish are to be expected; no site-specific species lists found in available sources beyond what would be typical for this reef system. The adjacent Car Pile area (accessible via extension from the same entry) hosts tarpon, moray eels, scorpionfish, octopus, and pufferfish — see Marie Pampoen site entry.

## Dive Profile

Pier entry adjacent to Ocean Encounters. Begin at the white mooring buoy at approximately 18 feet (6 metres). The wall descends steeply. Best marine life concentration is at 20–40 feet (6–12 m). Advanced divers can continue past 33 metres. The Car Pile artificial reef can be incorporated as an extension from the same entry.

## Entry and Exit

Resort guests use the Ocean Encounters pier adjacent to the dive shop. Non-resort divers access from Pierbaai public beach (a short walk around the resort perimeter). Ocean Encounters provides full equipment rental, guided dives, and air fills.

## Tips and Recommendations

- Non-resort divers should use Pierbaai beach access rather than trying to enter through the resort
- Best marine life is surprisingly shallow (6–12 m) — no need to push depth
- If current is present on arrival, assess carefully — strong surprise currents are reported here
- Combine with the Car Pile for a more varied dive from the same entry point

## Safety Considerations

Unexpected strong currents have been reported at this site — assess conditions before entering. Inexperienced divers should not dive here during current. A surface marker buoy is required; Ocean Encounters staff are present at this site.
