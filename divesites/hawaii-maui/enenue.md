## Enenue

Enenue is a wall dive site in Hawaii - Maui, Pacific.

## Overview

Enenue hugs the eastern inner wall of Molokini Crater, named after the Hawaiian chub (rudderfish) that school in the shallows, with a gentle slope from the surface to about 18 meters before the terrain steepens and the "Shark Condos" — a series of lava terraces harboring resting whitetip reef sharks — come into view around 40 meters. The shallower sections are rich with butterflyfish and moray eels, while deeper into the dive, boarfish and the occasional ray appear along the wall. This site is often dived as a live boat drift, allowing divers to cover more of the crater's inner wall and extending what is already a highly productive dive.

- **Site Type**: Wall dive
