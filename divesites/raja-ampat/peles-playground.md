## Pele's Playground

Located near Pele/Waaf island in northern Raja Ampat. Mobula rays, bait ball action, and hunting trevallies make this an exciting open-water site.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Raja Ampat North area, Raja Ampat, West Papua, Indonesia
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Advanced
- **Maximum Depth**: 25 meters
