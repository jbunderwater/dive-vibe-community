## Delos Cove

A sheltered lagoon dive near Balbulol Island in southeastern Misool. Shallow reef with good coral coverage and calm conditions.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Misool area, Raja Ampat, West Papua, Indonesia
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 17 meters
