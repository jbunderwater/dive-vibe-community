## Nudi Bomie

A coral bommie near Balbulol Island in southeastern Misool. A macro photographer's site with abundant nudibranchs and small critters on the bommie structure.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Misool area, Raja Ampat, West Papua, Indonesia
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 18 meters
