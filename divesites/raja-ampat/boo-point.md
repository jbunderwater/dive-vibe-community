## Boo Point

The eastern face of Boo island, with soft coral-draped walls and overhangs. Wobbegong sharks and epaulette sharks rest on ledges.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Misool area, Raja Ampat, West Papua, Indonesia
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Coral reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 30 meters
