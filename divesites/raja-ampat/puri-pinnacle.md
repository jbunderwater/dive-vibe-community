## Puri Pinnacle

A seamount with three outcrops topping at 5-7 metres in central Misool. Famous for massive anchovy and sardine bait balls attracting mobula rays and hunting trevallies. Also known as Anti-Chovies.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Misool area, Raja Ampat, West Papua, Indonesia
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Pinnacle/seamount
- **Difficulty Level**: Advanced
- **Maximum Depth**: 25 meters
