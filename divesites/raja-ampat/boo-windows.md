## Boo Windows

One of Misool's most iconic sites. Swim-through windows in the rock formation are carpeted with vibrant soft corals. Wobbegong sharks, epaulette sharks, and pygmy seahorses inhabit the overhangs.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Misool area, Raja Ampat, West Papua, Indonesia
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 30 meters
