## Toblerone

Shallow reef with a large north-south tunnel. Hard coral gardens and bommies on the reef top make this an accessible Misool dive.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Misool area, Raja Ampat, West Papua, Indonesia
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Coral reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 20 meters
