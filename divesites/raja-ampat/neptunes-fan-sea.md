## Neptune's Fan Sea

A current-swept channel between two islands. Sea fans spanning nearly 3 metres line the passage walls in the western Misool area.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Misool area, Raja Ampat, West Papua, Indonesia
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Drift dive
- **Difficulty Level**: Advanced
- **Maximum Depth**: 20 meters
