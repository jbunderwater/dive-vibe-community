## Farondi Cave

A sea tunnel bisects this island, exiting on a wall at 30 metres. A second cave at 15 metres features a soft coral-covered arch. One of Misool's most dramatic topographical dives.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Misool area, Raja Ampat, West Papua, Indonesia
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Cave/cavern
- **Difficulty Level**: Advanced
- **Maximum Depth**: 30 meters
