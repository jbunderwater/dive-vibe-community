## Love Potion No 9

An underwater ridge near Balbulol island. Current-fed soft corals and sea fans thrive along the ridge in the northern Misool area.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Misool area, Raja Ampat, West Papua, Indonesia
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Drift dive
- **Difficulty Level**: Advanced
- **Maximum Depth**: 25 meters
