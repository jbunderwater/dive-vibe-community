## Nudi Rock

A small island with surrounding pinnacles in the central Misool area. Abundant nudibranchs alongside pelagic visitors including barracuda, trevally, and grey reef sharks.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Misool area, Raja Ampat, West Papua, Indonesia
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Pinnacle/seamount
- **Difficulty Level**: Advanced
- **Maximum Depth**: 30 meters
