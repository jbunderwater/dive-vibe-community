## Tank Rock

A ridge near Fiabacet with grey sharks, groupers, and yellowfin barracuda. Fish density is so high they can obscure the coral below.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Misool area, Raja Ampat, West Papua, Indonesia
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Advanced
- **Maximum Depth**: 30 meters
