## Leisure Stroll Reef

A shallow, relaxed reef dive near Kalig Island in Misool. Maximum depth of just 7 metres makes this ideal for easy dives and snorkelling between deeper sites.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Misool area, Raja Ampat, West Papua, Indonesia
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Beginner
- **Maximum Depth**: 7 meters
