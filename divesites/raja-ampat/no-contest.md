## No Contest

Submerged pinnacles near Balbulol island with vertical walls covered in soft corals. Schools of batfish and bumphead parrotfish frequent the site.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Misool area, Raja Ampat, West Papua, Indonesia
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Pinnacle/seamount
- **Difficulty Level**: Advanced
- **Maximum Depth**: 30 meters
