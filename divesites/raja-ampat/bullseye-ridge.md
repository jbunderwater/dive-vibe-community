## Bullseye Ridge

An underwater ridge near Baturufus Island in the Penemu area. Pinnacle formation reaching 22 metres with healthy coral and reef fish.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Penemu area, Raja Ampat, West Papua, Indonesia
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Pinnacle/seamount
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 22 meters
