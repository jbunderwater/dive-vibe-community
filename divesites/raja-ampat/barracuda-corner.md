## Barracuda Corner

East side of Kalig Island in Misool. A wall dive reaching 28 metres where schools of barracuda gather at the current-swept corner.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Misool area, Raja Ampat, West Papua, Indonesia
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Wall dive
- **Difficulty Level**: Advanced
- **Maximum Depth**: 28 meters
