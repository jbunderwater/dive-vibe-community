## Andiamo

Two islets with a channel between them; pinnacle covered in soft coral with seasonal manta rays at a cleaning station in the Daram area of Misool.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Misool area, Raja Ampat, West Papua, Indonesia
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Pinnacle/seamount
- **Difficulty Level**: Advanced
- **Maximum Depth**: 30 meters
