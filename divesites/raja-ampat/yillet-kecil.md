## Yillet Kecil

A plateau with overhangs near the Boo area. Home to endemic Raja Ampat epaulette 'walking' sharks, making it an exceptional night dive site.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Misool area, Raja Ampat, West Papua, Indonesia
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 20 meters
