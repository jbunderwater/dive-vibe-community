## Macro Rock

A dedicated macro photography site in the Wagmab/Farondi area. Nudibranchs, pygmy seahorses, and an abundance of critters. Also known as Grouper Net.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Misool area, Raja Ampat, West Papua, Indonesia
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Advanced
- **Maximum Depth**: 25 meters
