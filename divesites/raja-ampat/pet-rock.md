## Pet Rock

Soft coral reef near Balbulol island in the northern Misool area.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Misool area, Raja Ampat, West Papua, Indonesia
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Advanced
- **Maximum Depth**: 25 meters
