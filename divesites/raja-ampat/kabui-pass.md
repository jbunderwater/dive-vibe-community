## Kabui Pass

A drift dive through the passage between Gam and Waigeo islands. Tidal currents carry divers through a scenic channel reaching 15 metres, with mangrove-lined walls and soft corals.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Kabui Bay area, Raja Ampat, West Papua, Indonesia
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Drift dive
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 15 meters
