## Warna Berwarna

The name means 'colourful' in Indonesian, and the site delivers -- stunning pink and orange soft corals carpet this reef near Fiabacet.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Misool area, Raja Ampat, West Papua, Indonesia
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Advanced
- **Maximum Depth**: 25 meters
