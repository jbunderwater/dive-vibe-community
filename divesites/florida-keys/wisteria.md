## Wisteria

Wisteria is a wreck dive site in the Florida Keys.

## Overview

Wisteria Island (also known locally as Christmas Tree Island) is a small uninhabited federal island created by US Navy dredging of Key West harbor in the late 1890s and early 1900s, located about 645 yards northwest of Key West. Historical records confirm old ship remnants on the island's north side, but the identity, condition, and dive character of this wreck site could not be established from any available public sources as of May 2026. No dive operator currently lists it in their standard rotation; any visit should be coordinated directly with a Key West dive shop.
