## CN Barges (3), c. 1941

Three Canadian National Railway barges dating to approximately 1941 rest in outer Halifax Harbour at approximately 24 metres. The barges are listed in the Halifax Harbour-area shipwreck records. Their individual names, exact dimensions, and the circumstances under which they came to rest at these coordinates are not confirmed in sources found in this survey. Bedford Basin and outer Halifax Harbour were used extensively during WWII as staging areas for convoys, and various service barges were retired or lost during this period.
