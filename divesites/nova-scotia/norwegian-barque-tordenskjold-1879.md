## Norwegian barque Tordenskjold (1879)

The Norwegian barque *Tordenskjold* is documented in Halifax Harbour-area shipwreck records as an 1879 wreck in the outer Halifax Harbour / McNabs Island area, resting in approximately 29 metres. The vessel's name honours the Danish-Norwegian naval hero Peter Tordenskiold (1690–1720); barques of this era were common in Scandinavian-Atlantic trade. The specific circumstances of the sinking — cargo, route, and cause — have not been confirmed in sources accessible to this survey.
