## Wellesly Rock

Wellesly Rock is a submerged rock formation at 14-18 meters providing good shallow-moderate diving for intermediate divers building Atlantic experience. The site offers stable conditions, moderate currents, and reliable visibility. A popular intermediate training location.

## Overview

Wellesly Rock is a reef dive site in Nova Scotia, North America. Cold Atlantic waters (4-18°C year-round) require proper thermal protection and training. The site features typical Atlantic diving characteristics with variable currents, good marine life encounters, and dramatic underwater topography.

## Marine Life

Regional marine life in Nova Scotia waters includes Atlantic wolffish, cunner, Atlantic cod, rock crab, green sea urchin, northern sea star, and cold-water anemones. Kelp beds are common in shallower rocky areas. No site-specific species reports were found for this site.
