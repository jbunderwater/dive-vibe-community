## Round Reef

Round Reef is a distinctive circular reef formation at 16-20 meters in the Bay of Fundy experiencing strong tidal influence. The site offers moderate intermediate diving with interesting topography and dynamic water conditions. The circular shape creates unique current patterns and marine life concentration.

## Overview

Round Reef is a reef dive site in Nova Scotia, North America. Cold Atlantic waters (4-18°C year-round) require proper thermal protection and training. The site features typical Atlantic diving characteristics with variable currents, good marine life encounters, and dramatic underwater topography.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Nova Scotia, North America
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate

## Marine Life

Regional marine life in Nova Scotia waters includes Atlantic wolffish, cunner, Atlantic cod, rock crab, green sea urchin, northern sea star, and cold-water anemones. Kelp beds are common in shallower rocky areas. No site-specific species reports were found for this site.

Access by boat. No dedicated dive charter service was identified for this site in available sources. Contact local Nova Scotia dive operators for current access information.
