## Hudson bomber plane (1944)

A Lockheed Hudson maritime patrol aircraft, dated 1944, is recorded in the Halifax Harbour-area shipwreck dataset at these coordinates. The RCAF operated Lockheed Hudsons from RCAF Station Dartmouth during WWII on Atlantic anti-submarine patrols. The specific circumstances of this aircraft entering the water — serial number, crew fate, and incident details — have not been confirmed in sources found in this survey. Note: a 1944 RCAF Lockheed aircraft (later identified as a Ventura, not a Hudson) made a forced landing on a frozen lake in rural Nova Scotia, not at this location; those are different incidents. No dive operator or aviation history source was found describing a Hudson wreck in Halifax Harbour at these coordinates.

**Location note:** The coordinates (44.6233°N, 63.5260°W) place this in outer Halifax Harbour near McNabs Island / Dartmouth, not the Shubenacadie Channel.
