## British tanker Kars (1942)

The British tanker Kars is a historically significant World War II-era wreck sunk in 1942. Located in the Shubenacadie Channel at 28 meters, this vessel rests in cold Atlantic water with strong tidal currents. The wreck retains good structural preservation and offers exploration of a significant historical artifact from the wartime transatlantic trade. This is an excellent site for advanced divers interested in maritime history.
