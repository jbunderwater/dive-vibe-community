## LSM-[??]

**Location note:** This wreck is in the **Musquash Estuary, New Brunswick** (not Nova Scotia), near the USS LSM-56 cluster. The OSM coordinates (45.1914°N, 66.2664°W) place it adjacent to the LSM-56.

This is an unidentified LSM (Landing Ship Medium) that was abandoned around 1960 in the Musquash Estuary near Saint John, New Brunswick, alongside the USS LSM-56 and what Wikipedia identifies as a third capsized LSM. The identity of this vessel has not been confirmed in available sources. Its OSM WikiData tag links to the general Landing Ship Medium Wikipedia article rather than a specific vessel. The hull is visible at low tide and rests in the intertidal/shallow subtidal zone in the same conditions as LSM-56.
