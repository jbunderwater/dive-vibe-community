## Lauderdale-by-the-Sea Beach

Lauderdale-by-the-Sea Beach is a reef dive site in South Florida, North America.

## Overview

Primary beach entry point for Lauderdale-by-the-Sea's renowned shore diving, offering direct access to shallow reef systems. Depths of 5 feet (1.5m) at entry, extending to 12-18 feet (3.7-5.5m) at the reef structure. Perfect beginner destination with excellent visibility and minimal current exposure. Access via Datura Avenue provides entry to the broader Lauderdale-by-the-Sea reef system within 100-400 feet of shore. Abundant tropical reef fish and marine life with protected water conditions.

## Site Information

- **Location**: South Florida, North America
- **Entry Type**: Shore entry
- **Site Type**: Coral reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Beginner
- **Maximum Depth**: 5 meters
