## Haulover Ledge

Haulover Ledge is a reef dive site in South Florida, North America.

## Overview

Ledge reef system in the Haulover area north of Miami. Depths range from 20-30 feet (6-9m) with some areas reaching 60 feet (18m). Strong tidal currents of 4-6 knots make this an intermediate-level site best suited for current-aware divers. Marine life includes snapper, roaming grouper, hogfish, and various reef fish. Part of a broader North Miami reef network providing excellent diving 10 minutes north of Haulover Inlet. North Canyon/Deep Trench area provides current relief and shelter.
