## Deep Turbo

An Advanced-only pinnacle dive on the north/northeast side of Gili Trawangan — coral mounds and sea mounds rising from a sandy bottom, to 35 meters, with bumphead parrotfish, spotted eagle rays, and reef sharks.

## Overview

Deep Turbo is a pinnacle/seamount site located north/northeast of Gili Trawangan, reserved for Advanced Open Water divers and above. Nitrox is strongly recommended. The site consists of coral mounds and sea mounds rising from a sandy bottom — Oceans 5 Dive describes thousands of garden eels in the sandy patches between sea mounds. Manta Dive records a depth range of 18–35 m; Blue Marlin Dive records 22–32 m for the Turbo complex. Strong currents are characteristic. The marine life list at this site is particularly rich: bumphead parrotfish, spotted eagle rays, white and blacktip reef sharks, green turtles, and gorgonian sea fans have all been confirmed, in addition to pygmy seahorses and garden eels.

## Site Information

- **Location**: North/northeast of Gili Trawangan, Gili Islands / Lombok
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Pinnacle/seamount
- **Difficulty Level**: Advanced
- **Maximum Depth**: 35 meters
- **Current**: Strong to very strong; always present
- **Best Time**: May to October

## Marine Life

Confirmed: bumphead parrotfish, spotted eagle rays, white and blacktip reef sharks, green turtles, pygmy seahorses, gorgonian sea fans, garden eels in sandy patches between sea mounds (Oceans 5 Dive), batfish, and barracuda. Strong current concentrates schooling fish at the mound peaks.

## Safety Considerations

Advanced Open Water certification required. Strong current — always coordinate pickup with the boat before descending. Nitrox strongly recommended for no-decompression margin at 35 m. Do not descend beyond planned depth.
