## Deep Turbo

An Advanced-level pinnacle dive on the northeast side of Gili Trawangan — large sea mounds with strong current, sharks, rays, and garden eels to 30 meters.

## Overview

Deep Turbo is a pinnacle/seamount site on the northeast side of Gili Trawangan, reserved for Advanced Open Water divers and above. Trawangan Dive describes it as 16–30 meters with large sea mounds; Oceans 5 Dive confirms large sea mounds with thousands of garden eels. Blue Marlin Dive records a depth range of 22–32 meters for the Turbo site complex. Strong currents are characteristic of this site. Marine life confirmed by Trawangan Dive includes batfish schools, red-tooth triggerfish, snapper, blue-spotted rays, sharks, pygmy seahorses, and nudibranchs.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Northeast Gili Trawangan, Gili Islands / Lombok
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Pinnacle
- **Difficulty Level**: Advanced
- **Maximum Depth**: 30 meters
- **Current**: Strong to very strong; always present
- **Best Time**: May to October

## Marine Life

Confirmed by Trawangan Dive: batfish schools, red-tooth triggerfish, snapper, blue-spotted stingrays, sharks, pygmy seahorses, nudibranchs. Garden eels in sandy patches between sea mounds (Oceans 5 Dive). Barracuda and rays in open water (Oceans 5 Dive).

## Safety Considerations

Advanced Open Water certification required. Strong current. Always carry an SMB and coordinate pickup before descent. Do not descend beyond planned depth.
