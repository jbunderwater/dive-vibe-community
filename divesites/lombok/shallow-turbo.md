## Shallow Turbo

A beginner-friendly drift site in the Gili Islands channel — part of the Turbo site complex with Biorock structures, turtles, and schooling fish.

## Overview

Shallow Turbo is the shallower section of the Turbo site complex, located in the Gili Islands channel. Trawangan Dive describes it as an all-levels site with drift conditions and Biorock structures. Confirmed marine life (Trawangan Dive): turtles, schooling fish, unicorn fish, scorpionfish, trumpet fish, and ornate ghost pipefish. Blue Marlin Dive records the Turbo complex depth range as 22m avg, 32m max — the Shallow Turbo section operates in the shallower range. Oceans 5 Dive lists it as a beginner drift.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Gili Islands channel, accessible from Lombok
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Drift dive
- **Difficulty Level**: Beginner
- **Maximum Depth**: 18 meters
- **Current**: Gentle to moderate drift
- **Best Time**: May to October

## Marine Life

Confirmed by Trawangan Dive: turtles, schooling fish, unicorn fish, scorpionfish, trumpet fish, ornate ghost pipefish. Biorock structures in the area support additional fish communities.

## Safety Considerations

Drift requires SMB carry and drift pickup procedure. Do not swim upstream against the current. Always dive with a buddy.
