## Frog Fish Point

A muck diving site near Gili Air — sandy and rubble substrate with frogfish as the signature species. Coordinates may be approximate.

## Overview

Frog Fish Point is a muck-style dive site in the Gili Air area, confirmed as a sandy and rubble macro site with frogfish as the primary draw. Blue Marlin Gili Air confirms "clown frogfish" at this site. Gili Air Divers confirms "multiple frogfish species." No source confirms the three-species list (warty, giant, and hairy frogfish) as a complete and accurate record for this specific location.

Coordinate note: The OSM data includes the note "Location might have to be checked with GPS" — the listed coordinates should be treated as approximate.

- **Location**: Near Gili Air, Gili Islands / Lombok (coordinates approximate)

## Marine Life

Frogfish confirmed on sandy and rubble substrate — species include clown frogfish (Blue Marlin Gili Air) and multiple frogfish species (Gili Air Divers). Specific additional species are not confirmed from 2+ sources for this exact site.

## Safety Considerations

Maintain positive buoyancy above sandy substrate — stonefish and scorpionfish can be present on muck sites. Do not stir the sand — visibility collapses instantly. Always carry an SMB.
