## Καλλιθέα

Καλλιθέα (Kalithea) is a cave and cavern dive site in Rhodes, Greece, set within a sheltered bay beneath the restored Italian-era Kalithea Spa.

## Overview

Kalithea Bay is a sheltered cove located a few kilometres south of Rhodes Town on the island of Rhodes, Dodecanese. The site sits within the grounds of the historic Kalithea Spa (Thermes Kalitheas), a 1920s–30s Italian-built thermal bath complex now protected as a cultural heritage site. Kalithea means "beautiful view" in Greek.

The underwater terrain is the site's main attraction. The bay features caverns, swim-throughs, a blow-hole cave, and a mushroom cavern — shallow arch and overhang structures formed in the rocky shoreline that create dramatic filtered-light effects. The seafloor is sandy with rocky outcrops. Maximum depth is 10–15 metres, making this one of the most accessible dive sites in Rhodes and a popular location for introductory dives and Open Water students.

Divers access the site via a custom-built dive platform where the boat moors. Visibility typically reaches 20 metres in the clear Aegean water. Currents are none to very weak inside the sheltered bay. Water temperatures range from 19°C in winter to 30°C in summer.

## Dive Profile

- **Entry**: Boat to dedicated dive platform; some guided shore entry options
- **Depth range**: 5–15 m
- **Bottom**: Sandy, rocky outcrops
- **Conditions**: Sheltered; none to very weak current
- **Visibility**: 15–20 m typical
- **Best season**: June–mid-October (operator schedule)

## Marine Life

Confirmed species at this site include sea bream, damselfish, rock fish, flounder, pipefish, and octopus. Moray eels and grouper have been recorded in the deeper cavern sections. Occasional encounters with Mediterranean barracuda, monk seals, turtles, and nudibranchs are reported by operators but are not guaranteed.

## Photography

The cavern swim-throughs and light shafts through the mushroom cave structures create strong wide-angle opportunities. The shallow depth (5–15 m) allows excellent natural light throughout the dive. The Italian-era spa architecture above water provides additional topside subjects.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Kalithea, Rhodes, Dodecanese, South Aegean, Greece
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Cave/cavern
- **Difficulty Level**: Beginner
- **Maximum Depth**: 15 meters
