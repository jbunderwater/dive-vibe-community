# Wreck Rock

A wreck dive site in the southwest Isla Colón area of Bocas del Toro. Note: this site may correspond to the widely documented "Old Wreck" or Barco Hundido site; no independent source confirms "Wreck Rock" as a distinct named site.

## Overview

Wreck Rock is listed in this database at coordinates corresponding to the southwest area of Isla Colón. The best-documented wreck dive in this general area is known as "The Old Wreck," "The Wreck," or "Barco Hundido" — an old car ferry intentionally sunk to create an artificial reef. Multiple sources (divescover.com, thevisitorpanama.info, solbungalowsbocas.com) describe this wreck as lying upright in approximately 5–12 meters of water above a reef off Isla Colón's southernmost point.

No source under the name "Wreck Rock" was found across any major local dive operator website, regional dive database, or community forum during this research. It is possible this is a colloquial or alternate name for the Old Wreck/Barco Hundido site, or a locally-named rock formation adjacent to the wreck. The site cannot be described in detail without a confirming source.

The separately documented Barco Hundido (OSM node 12639496075) is listed as a distinct site in this database at nearby coordinates.
