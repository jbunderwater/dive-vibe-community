# Dolphins Bay

A sheltered bay (Laguna Bocatorito) south of the Bocas del Toro archipelago with a resident population of bottlenose dolphins — primarily a snorkelling and boat tour destination, with limited diving information available.

## Overview

Laguna Bocatorito, known as Dolphin Bay, is located approximately 12 km south of Bocas Town — about 15–20 minutes by boat. The bay is bounded by Cristóbal Island to the west and a mainland peninsula, and is well-known for its resident year-round population of bottlenose dolphins, including mothers with calves. The bay's protected waters and mangrove-fringed edges provide ideal habitat for the dolphins.

This site is primarily operated as a snorkelling and dolphin-watching boat tour destination. Multiple sources indicate that swimming may be restricted in certain parts of the bay to protect the dolphins. No source specifically documents this as a scuba diving site with reef at depth. The reef diving character and 10m depth listed in this entry are unconfirmed by independent sources — treat with caution and confirm with local operators before planning a dive.

## Marine Life

Bottlenose dolphins are the confirmed resident population — year-round, with best sightings June–July when rough seas in the open ocean drive dolphins into the calm bay. Mother dolphins with calves are characteristic of this location. No verified site-specific scuba diving marine life documentation has been found.

## Entry and Exit

Boat tour from Bocas Town operators. Check with local operators for current access rules regarding dolphin interaction and swimming within the bay. The site is 15–20 minutes by boat.

## Tips and Recommendations

Dolphin encounters are highly likely given the resident population — described as "pretty much guaranteed" to see dolphins. Best viewing is from the boat; swimming access depends on current regulations. If scuba diving is offered at this location, confirm depth, conditions, and marine life details with the operator directly.

## Safety Considerations

Follow local regulations for dolphin interaction. Do not enter the water without operator guidance regarding current bay access rules.

## Additional Resources

- Bocas del Toro boat tour operators: offer dolphin bay tours from Bocas Town
- Best dolphin sightings: June–July
