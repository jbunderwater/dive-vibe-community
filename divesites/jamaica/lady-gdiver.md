Lady G'Diver is a dive operator located at the Alligator Head Foundation property a few miles east of Port Antonio, on Jamaica's northeast coast. The operator runs dives across approximately eight miles of interconnected reefs and walls within the Alligator Head Foundation Fish and Reef Sanctuary, ranging from 30 to 110 feet (9–33 metres) depth. The operator takes no more than six divers per trip and all dives are drift dives. A named wreck dive operated by Lady G'Diver is the Paul Bogle Shipwreck — a 105-foot Jamaican coastguard patrol vessel deployed as an artificial reef in August 2023 at 60 feet (18 metres) depth.
