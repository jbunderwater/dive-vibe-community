## Aquarium

The Aquarium is an ultra-shallow reef in the Cousteau Reserve near Malendure, at maximum 6 metres depth. It is a companion site to Jardin de Corail and forms part of the sheltered inner face of Pigeon Island.

## Overview

Sitting in the protected waters of the Cousteau Reserve, this shallow reef is an ideal location for first dives, beginners, and snorkelers. The rocky and coral seabed reaches only 6 metres. The site is part of the same protected reserve system as Jardin de Corail and Piscine; diving here requires going with a licensed local operator.

Note: this "Aquarium" site near Malendure (part of the Cousteau Reserve) is distinct from "L'Aquarium" in Les Saintes, which is a wall/tunnel dive.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Cousteau Reserve, Pigeon Island area, Malendure, Basse-Terre, Guadeloupe
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Coral reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Beginner
- **Maximum Depth**: 6 metres

## Marine Life

Confirmed for the Cousteau Reserve area by reserve-cousteau.fr and Les Heures Saines: French angelfish, grey angelfish, parrotfish, barracuda, hawksbill turtles, green turtles, French grunts (gorettes), yellowtail snappers, blue tangs. These are documented for the reserve system. No additional site-specific species reports specific to this 6-metre Aquarium entry have been found beyond the reserve-wide documentation.

Regional marine life applies; no site-specific species reports found beyond general reserve documentation.

## Entry and Exit

Boat from Malendure; giant stride entry. Often combined with Jardin de Corail for a two-site day in the reserve. SMB on ascent due to channel boat traffic.
