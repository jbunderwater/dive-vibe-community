## Piscine

Piscine — "Swimming Pool" — is one of the most sheltered and shallowest dive sites in the Cousteau Reserve, formed in the natural protected zone between the twin Pigeon Island islets. Maximum depth is 6 metres.

## Overview

The name reflects the site's exceptionally calm, enclosed character. A rocky seabed with coral growth forms the bottom at 4–6 metres. The site is commonly used for discovery dives, first ocean dives, and beginner certifications. It is part of the Cousteau Reserve mooring buoy system.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Between the twin islets of Pigeon Island, Cousteau Reserve, Malendure, Guadeloupe
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Coral reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Beginner
- **Maximum Depth**: 6 metres

## Marine Life

Documented for the Cousteau Reserve by reserve-cousteau.fr: parrotfish, sergeant majors, wrasse, damselfish, hawksbill turtles. Juvenile fish use the sheltered water as nursery habitat. No site-specific species reports have been found for Piscine distinct from the reserve-wide documentation.

Regional marine life applies; no site-specific species reports found beyond general reserve documentation.
