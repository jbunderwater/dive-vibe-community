## La Caye

La Caye is an intermediate reef dive in the Malendure area, offered by Les Heures Saines as a regular site and also featured as a night dive destination.

## Overview

La Caye is listed by Les Heures Saines as one of their regular dive sites. It is featured specifically as a night dive location, described by Les Heures Saines as an intimate atmosphere with 60-minute dives revealing corals and nocturnal creatures. It is situated in the Malendure/Bouillante area, south of the main Pigeon Island reserve sites, and can reach 25 metres at its deepest.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Malendure area, Basse-Terre, Guadeloupe
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 25 meters

## Marine Life

Confirmed by Les Heures Saines for this site: corals, sea turtles, astrophyton (basket stars), and corallimorphaire (corallimorph anemones) during night dives. These nocturnal species are specifically noted for La Caye night dives.

Note: "Caribbean reef sharks," "eagle rays," and "garden eels" attributed to La Caye in a previous version are not confirmed by sources specific to this site and have been removed.

Regional marine life applies; beyond night dive species confirmed above, no additional site-specific day-dive marine life reports have been found.

## Night Dive

La Caye is recommended specifically as a night dive site by Les Heures Saines. Basket stars (astrophyton) and corallimorph anemones extend their feeding structures at night, making the site significantly different from daytime.
