## Tumbo Reef

Tumbo Reef is a boat-accessed dive site in the San Juan Islands offering rocky reef formations typical of northern Puget Sound diving. This moderately shallow reef features cold-water marine species and scenic underwater topography.

## Overview

Tumbo Reef is a rocky reef dive site located in the San Juan Islands area of northern Puget Sound. The site features bottom depths typical of 20-40 feet (6-12 meters) with excellent biodiversity. Water temperatures around 50°F (10°C) year-round require proper thermal protection. Typical visibility ranges from 15-40 feet depending on season and tidal conditions. This site is accessible by boat and offers good diving throughout the year.
