# Vista Blue

*A southern Bonaire shore reef in the exposed southwest corridor between Sweet Dreams and Red Beryl — intermediate conditions with a good coral slope and sea fan formations.*

## Overview

Vista Blue is on Bonaire's southwest coast, in the less-visited stretch south of the main tourist belt near Sweet Dreams and Red Beryl. The site offers a gradual reef slope with good sea fan cover and the clearer water typical of the southern coast. The intermediate difficulty reflects the somewhat more exposed southwest location compared to the sheltered central sites.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Southwest Bonaire, southern coast
- **Entry Type**: Shore entry
- **Site Type**: Reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 30 meters
- **Typical Visibility**: 15–25 metres
- **Current**: Light to moderate
- **Best Time**: Morning

## Marine Life

Parrotfish, angelfish, grunts, sea fans, soft corals, turtles, and moray eels. The southwest coast reef supports good diversity in relatively undisturbed conditions.

## Dive Profile

Shore entry and gradual slope to 30 metres. Most activity in the 8–22 metre range.

## Entry and Exit

Shore entry from the southern coast road. Exit at the same point.

## Tips

- Combine with Sweet Dreams and Red Beryl for a southern half-day

## Safety

Intermediate conditions. Light-to-moderate current possible. Carry an SMB given the exposed location.

## Photography

Good sea fan and soft coral subjects. Clear southern water.

## Nearby Sites

Sweet Dreams is to the south. Red Beryl and Atlantis are to the north.
