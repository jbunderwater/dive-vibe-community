# Sweet Dreams

*A southern Bonaire reef site on the remote southwest coast between Willemstoren Lighthouse and Vista Blue — Advanced due to exposure and isolation rather than depth.*

## Overview

Sweet Dreams is on Bonaire's remote southwest coast, in the exposed stretch south of the main tourist belt where the road becomes rougher. The site sits between Vista Blue to the north and Willemstoren Lighthouse to the south, sharing the somewhat exposed character of the southern-tip diving. The reef is a gradual slope with healthy corals and the clearer water typical of the less-dived southern stretches. Advanced difficulty reflects the isolated location, the potential for current, and the absence of nearby support.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Southwest Bonaire, southern coast between Vista Blue and Willemstoren
- **Entry Type**: Shore entry
- **Site Type**: Reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Advanced
- **Maximum Depth**: 30 meters
- **Typical Visibility**: 15–25 metres
- **Current**: Light to moderate; can be stronger on windier days
- **Best Time**: Early morning in calm conditions

## Marine Life

Parrotfish, angelfish, groupers, barracuda, and turtles. The sponge-covered slope supports typical Caribbean reef species with the cleaner water of the less-dived south coast.

## Dive Profile

Shore entry and gradual reef slope to 30 metres. More productive in the 10–22 metre range.

## Entry and Exit

Shore entry from the southern coastal road. Rocky/sandy shore. Exit at the same point.

## Tips

- Early morning for calmest conditions
- Carry a dive flag and SMB given the exposed location
- The isolation means no nearby help — be self-sufficient

## Safety

Advanced due to isolation and current potential. Assess conditions from shore before entry. Carry an SMB.

## Photography

The clear southern water and quieter reef offer good photography in calm conditions.

## Nearby Sites

Vista Blue is to the north. Willemstoren Lighthouse is to the south.
