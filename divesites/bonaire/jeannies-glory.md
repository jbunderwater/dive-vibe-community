# Jeannie's Glory

*A southern Bonaire shore reef in the Salt City–Alice in Wonderland corridor, with a typical fringing reef slope and good fish diversity.*

## Overview

Jeannie's Glory is a southern Bonaire shore site on the west coast between Salt City to the north and Alice in Wonderland to the south. The site offers straightforward fringing reef diving typical of the southern coast, with a gradual slope and the reef health that characterises the Bonaire National Marine Park. It is a convenient dive in the busy southern corridor.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Southern Bonaire west coast
- **Entry Type**: Shore entry
- **Site Type**: Reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 30 meters
- **Typical Visibility**: 20–30 metres
- **Current**: Usually light
- **Best Time**: Morning

## Marine Life

Southern Bonaire reef species: parrotfish, angelfish, snapper, grunts, and groupers. Turtles are seen along this stretch of coast. Moray eels in reef crevices.

## Dive Profile

Shore entry and gradual slope to 30 metres. Most productive in the 8–25 metre range.

## Entry and Exit

Shore entry from roadside. Exit at the same point.

## Tips

- Convenient for combining with nearby southern sites on the same day

## Safety

Intermediate depth. Standard precautions.

## Photography

Standard southern Bonaire reef photography.

## Nearby Sites

Salt City is to the north. Larry's Lair is also nearby. Alice in Wonderland is to the south.
