# Soft Coral Garden (unmarked site)

*An unmarked "dive like a local" site on the far southern Bonaire coast — named for a soft coral community on the southern fringing reef, not on the official BNMP marker system.*

## Overview

Soft Coral Garden is an unmarked dive site on Bonaire's southern west coast, between Sweet Dreams and the Red Slave area. The name references a soft coral community on this section of the fringing reef. Like the other unmarked southern sites in this cluster (Yellow Hut, Hidden Beach), it does not appear in the official BNMP numbered-marker system or on standard tourist dive-site maps. It is a "dive like a local" site known primarily to experienced local divers and repeat visitors to the southern coast. The intermediate difficulty reflects the exposed southwest location.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Far southern Bonaire, southwest coast
- **Entry Type**: Shore entry
- **Site Type**: Reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 25 meters
- **Typical Visibility**: 15–25 metres
- **Current**: Light to moderate
- **Best Time**: Morning

## Marine Life

Soft corals and gorgonians on the southern fringing reef. Specific species unconfirmed from independent sources.

## Dive Profile

Shore entry to a reef slope. The soft coral community is presumably in the shallower part of the reef (5–15 metres range), consistent with good light penetration for soft coral growth.

## Entry and Exit

Shore entry from the southern coastal road. Not marked with a BNMP yellow stone. Exit at the same point.

## Tips

- Local knowledge helpful for locating the entry
- Best combined with nearby official sites

## Safety

Southwest exposure. Intermediate conditions. Carry an SMB.

## Photography

The soft coral community would be a primary photographic subject if conditions are right.

## Nearby Sites

Sweet Dreams and Red Slave are nearby to the south. Yellow Hut and Hidden Beach are adjacent unmarked sites.
