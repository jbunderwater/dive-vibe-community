# Corporal Meiss

*Also known as North Belnem — a central Bonaire shore reef between Bachelor's Beach and Windsock, with easy conditions and straightforward access from the coastal road.*

## Overview

Corporal Meiss (alt. name: North Belnem) is a central Bonaire shore site on the lower west coast, in the busy dive belt south of Kralendijk between Windsock and Bachelor's Beach. The site shares the fringing reef character of the central west coast: easy shore entry, a gradual reef slope, and calm conditions typical of Bonaire's protected leeward side. It is used frequently as a between-dives fill-in site or a morning warm-up given its accessible location.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Central Bonaire, BNMP corridor south of Kralendijk
- **Entry Type**: Shore entry
- **Site Type**: Coral reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Beginner
- **Maximum Depth**: 30 metres
- **Typical Visibility**: 20–30 metres
- **Current**: Usually light
- **Best Time**: Morning to afternoon

## Marine Life

Standard central Bonaire reef community: parrotfish, angelfish, grunts, snapper, and groupers on the reef slope. Turtles are seen along this stretch of coast. Macro subjects in the encrusting reef.

## Dive Profile

Shore entry and gradual reef slope. Most productive life in the 8–20 metre range. Sandy bottom at depth.

## Entry and Exit

Shore entry via standard western coast ironshore. Exit at the same point.

## Tips

- Convenient for central coast touring with other nearby sites in the same corridor

## Safety

Easy site. Monitor depth on the outer slope.

## Photography

Standard fringing reef photography opportunity in good visibility.

## Nearby Sites

Windsock is to the north. Bachelor's Beach is to the south. Salt Pier and Angel City are further south.
