## Brassball Wreck

Brassball Wreck is one of St. Kitts' shallowest, most accessible wreck dives, sitting in less than 25 feet (8 metres) of water.

## Overview

The Brassball Wreck's shallow depth of under 25 feet makes it a popular choice for beginner divers and snorkelers alike. Beyond the confirmed depth and its reputation as an easy, accessible site, detailed information about the vessel's history and layout is not available from the sources checked for this entry.
