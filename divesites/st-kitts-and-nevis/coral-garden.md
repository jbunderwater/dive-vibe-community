## Coral Garden

Coral Garden sits about 3km (2 miles) west of the Four Seasons Resort on Nevis, a coral reef reaching a maximum depth of 21 metres.

## Overview

Coral Garden is one of the named dive sites regularly run by Nevis-based operators near the Four Seasons Resort, alongside the nearby sulfur-vent site known as Champagne Garden. The reef reaches a maximum depth of 21 metres (69 feet) and is rated suitable for both certified and resort-course divers. Beyond the confirmed depth, location, and the two species noted below, more detailed site-specific description was not available from the sources checked for this entry — the previous version of this page listed several species and features that could not be traced back to any source and have been removed.

## Site Information

- **Location**: 3km (2 miles) west of Four Seasons Resort, Nevis
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Beginner
- **Maximum Depth**: 21 meters

## Marine Life

Confirmed sightings at this site are schools of Atlantic spadefish and large sea fans. No other species have been verified as specific to Coral Garden; St. Kitts and Nevis's reefs more broadly support turtles, stingrays, moray eels, and Caribbean lobsters, but these have not been confirmed at this particular site.
