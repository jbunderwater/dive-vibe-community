## Rusty Barge Wreck

Rusty Barge Wreck is a shallow wreck accessible by boat along the Belize Barrier Reef, sitting in approximately 8 metres of water.

## Overview

The Rusty Barge Wreck is a rusted-out barge hull resting in about 8 metres of water inside the Belize Barrier Reef. The hull is decorated with soft corals and encrusting sponges and serves as a permanent home for a small school of snappers. The shallow depth makes this a suitable dive for newly certified divers and underwater photographers who want extended bottom time; moray eels, small groupers, and cleaner shrimp are typical barge wreck residents in these Caribbean waters. Wave surge and current are possible through cuts in the reef when conditions are not calm. No named vessel history has been confirmed for this structure; it is an unnamed abandoned barge.
