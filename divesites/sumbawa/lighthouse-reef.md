## Lighthouse Reef

Sheltered muck and macro diving site in a bay on the northeast side of Sangeang Island. Black volcanic sand slopes mixed with colorful coral garden. Premier critter diving with frogfish, nudibranchs, boxer crabs, pipefishes, pygmy seahorses, cuttlefish, and flame file shells with their signature electric-flash light displays. Calm conditions with minimal current make it suitable for less experienced divers compared to other Sangeang sites.

## Site Information

- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Muck dive
- **Difficulty Level**: Beginner
- **Maximum Depth**: 25 meters
