## Rollercoaster

Rollercoaster is an advanced current dive at the northwest tip of Gili Banta (Banta Island), an uninhabited volcanic island situated between east Sumbawa and Komodo National Park. The site takes its name from the undulating underwater topography and the unpredictable push of vertical and horizontal currents.

## Overview

Rollercoaster sits at the most exposed, current-swept corner of Gili Banta. The site features undulating walls and steep slopes to 30 m, swept by both vertical and horizontal currents that bring plankton-rich water from the Sape Strait. The cold upwellings from Flores mean temperatures can drop noticeably compared to the rest of Sumbawa — an extra exposure layer is recommended. Large rock formations and deep canyons attract a variety of sharks including grey reefs, whitetips, and blacktips, as well as schooling pelagics: giant trevally, big-eyed jacks, and tuna. The strong, variable currents require Advanced certification and experience with negative entries. Lanyards for camera rigs are advisable. Day trips depart from Kalimaya Dive Resort (approximately 40 minutes by speedboat) or by liveaboard. Best season April to November.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Northwest tip of Gili Banta, Sumbawa / Komodo border
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Advanced
- **Maximum Depth**: 30 meters
- **Currents**: Strong — vertical and horizontal; cold upwellings possible
- **Best Season**: April–November

## Marine Life

Grey reef sharks, whitetip sharks, blacktip sharks, giant trevally, big-eyed jacks, yellowfin tuna, barracuda.

## Dive Profile

Negative entry is common given currents. Follow the undulating walls and slopes, using rock formations for shelter when needed. Depths to 30 m; pelagic action often in mid-water above the reef. Hold camera rigs securely in variable current.

## Safety Notes

Cold upwellings can surprise divers — plan for possible thermoclines and carry adequate exposure protection. Strong current requires current hook or reef hook readiness. Advanced divers only.
