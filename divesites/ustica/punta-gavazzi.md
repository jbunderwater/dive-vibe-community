## Punta Gavazzi

Punta Gavazzi is a reef dive site in Ustica, Europe.

## Overview

Punta Gavazzi is an archaeological dive site off Ustica's southeastern coast, featuring a signposted underwater trail created by archaeologist Sebastiano Tusa. Lead anchors dating from the 3rd century BC to the 4th century AD and amphora fragments from various periods lie on the volcanic lava rock seabed at a maximum depth of 20 metres. A lighthouse stands above the dive site on the headland. The site's archaeological character reflects Ustica's position along ancient Sicilian Channel trading routes.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Ustica, Europe
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 20 meters
