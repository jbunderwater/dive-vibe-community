## Banc des Gorgones

Banc des Gorgones (Gorgonian Bank) is one of the first dive sites discovered near Nosy Be, a two-level coral plateau famous for its massive sea fans and macro life.

## Overview

Located approximately 13 km from Ambatoloaka (about 40 minutes by boat), Banc des Gorgones is a coral plateau arranged at two depth levels: the main plateau at 21 metres and an upper section at 15 metres, separated by a garden of garden eels. The site is best dived on an ebb tide. The distinguishing feature is the abundance of large gorgonian sea fans — some three metres across — that cover the plateau. Sharp-eyed divers regularly find ghost pipefish, seahorses, nudibranchs, and lobsters hiding among the fans. Hawksbill turtles are resident at the site. Schools of pelagic fish pass over the plateau, and the macro-photography opportunities are considered among the best around Nosy Be.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Offshore Ambatoloaka, Nosy Be, Madagascar
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 22 meters
