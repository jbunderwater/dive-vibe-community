## Shark Point

Shark Point is a dramatic ocean wall dive located 21 km offshore from Nosy Be, one of the region's premier pelagic encounters.

## Overview

Shark Point is a deep wall dive beginning at 18 metres and dropping well past 50 metres into the blue of the Mozambique Channel. The site is named for the reliable congregation of grey reef sharks, blacktip reef sharks, and whitetip reef sharks that patrol the drop-off. Whale sharks and occasional hammerhead sharks have also been reported here. Large schools of fusiliers and snappers school along the wall face. The site is best approached on a rising tide when visibility is at its best and shark activity is highest. Due to depth and potential currents, solid buoyancy control is required.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Offshore Nosy Be, Madagascar, Africa
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Wall dive
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 30 meters
