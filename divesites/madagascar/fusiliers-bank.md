## Fusiliers Bank

Fusiliers Bank is a shallow reef plateau behind Nosy Sakatia, named for the dense schools of fusiliers that dominate the site and suited to all experience levels.

## Overview

Fusiliers Bank sits at 15–18 metres, located approximately 10 km behind Nosy Sakatia from Nosy Be. The plateau is characterised by exceptional visibility and moderate currents. True to its name, the site is dominated by enormous aggregations of fusilier fish, which form shimmering curtains over the reef. Snappers, emperors, and humphead parrotfish pass through regularly. The shallower depth and generally calm conditions make it accessible to beginner divers, while the density of fish life provides interest for all levels. It also serves as a good macro-photography site, with opportunities to find smaller reef creatures among the coral structures. Dive operators in Nosy Be commonly include it as a second dive alongside deeper sites.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Behind Nosy Sakatia, Nosy Be area, Madagascar
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Beginner
- **Maximum Depth**: 18 meters
