# Tuna Farm

Dive inside a tuna pen off Comino — surrounded by hundreds of massive bluefin tuna in crystal-clear water.

## Overview

Tuna Farm (Comino) offers the same unique experience as the Qawra tuna pens — diving inside large circular net enclosures containing hundreds of bluefin tuna. Divers descend into the pen and are surrounded by tuna ranging 1–2 metres long. The tuna swimming in one direction creates a vortex effect. The Comino location benefits from the crystal-clear waters of the channel between Malta and Gozo. Boat access only.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Offshore, Comino channel
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Coral reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Advanced
- **Maximum Depth**: 35 meters
- **Typical Visibility**: 15–30 meters
- **Current**: Light to moderate
- **Best Time**: May to October

## Marine Life

Hundreds of bluefin tuna, ranging 1–2 metres long. The crystal-clear Comino channel waters provide excellent visibility for the encounter.

## Tips and Recommendations

- Check conditions with local operators before diving
- Carry a torch for overhangs and cave features
- Dive within your certification limits
