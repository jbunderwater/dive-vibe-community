# The Canyon

A natural underwater canyon with reef walls, a sandy floor, and even a sunken car.

## Overview

The Canyon is a natural underwater canyon formation near the Marfa/Qawra area with reef walls on both sides. One wall drops to 17 metres on top and 28 metres on the outer side, leading to a large sand field at 35 metres. The canyon itself sits at approximately 20–23 metres depth. A wreck of an Opel Ascona car also lies in the area. Note: there is a separate 'Canyon' dive site on Comino — these are different locations.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Marfa/Qawra area, north Malta
- **Entry Type**: Shore entry
- **Site Type**: Coral reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 20 meters
- **Typical Visibility**: 15–30 meters
- **Current**: Light to moderate
- **Best Time**: May to October

## Marine Life

Reef fish variety, bonito, groupers, and stingrays are possible encounters. The canyon walls support typical Mediterranean reef species.

## Tips and Recommendations

- Check conditions with local operators before diving
- Carry a torch for overhangs and cave features
- Dive within your certification limits
