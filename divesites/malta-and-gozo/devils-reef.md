# Devil's Reef

A long reef with wall structures, caves with stalactites, and a swim-through at 25 metres — dropping to extreme depths.

## Overview

Devil's Reef (Qammieh Point) is located at the western end of Marfa Ridge (Ras il-Qammieh) on Malta's north coast. The site features a shallow 4–6 metre plateau giving way to wall structures with caves and caverns, including a swim-through at 25 metres. An old cave on the southern side contains stalactites. The long reef runs approximately 900 metres westward. A sandy slope beyond the wall descends to extreme depths (70+ metres). Access is by boat only.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Ras il-Qammieh (Marfa Ridge), north Malta
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Wall dive
- **Difficulty Level**: Advanced
- **Maximum Depth**: 50 meters
- **Typical Visibility**: 15–30 meters
- **Current**: Light to moderate
- **Best Time**: May to October

## Marine Life

Morays noted among the cave and crevice systems. The deep wall attracts pelagic visitors. Marine life quantity varies with conditions.

## Tips and Recommendations

- Check conditions with local operators before diving
- Carry a torch for overhangs and cave features
- Dive within your certification limits
