# Għar ta' Ras il-Qamħ

Boulders, caves, and swim-throughs near Cathedral Cave — best combined as a multi-site Ghasri Valley dive.

## Overview

Għar ta' Ras il-Qamħ (Cave of Wheat), also known as Ras il-Kanun or Ta Lenk, features boulders and caves with swim-throughs beneath large rock formations near Ghasri Valley on Gozo's west coast. The site combines cave, reef, and wall elements with an average depth of 10 metres and maximum of 30+ metres. Shore access via the Cathedral Cave ladder at Ghasri is possible. The site is best combined with Cathedral Cave for a comprehensive dive.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Ghasri Valley area, west coast Gozo
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Cave/cavern
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 20 meters
- **Typical Visibility**: 20–30 meters
- **Current**: Light to moderate
- **Best Time**: May to October

## Marine Life

Typical Gozo reef species in the boulder and cave habitats.

## Tips and Recommendations

- Best combined with Cathedral Cave for a full Ghasri dive
- Multiple swim-throughs beneath large boulders
- Shore access via Cathedral Cave ladder
