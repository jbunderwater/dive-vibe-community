# St. George's Bay

An urban shore dive in the heart of St. Julian's — convenient hotel-based diving with nearby Merkanti Reef.

## Overview

St. George's Bay is a Blue Flag certified beach in the St. Julian's/Paceville area on Malta's northeast coast. Multiple PADI dive centres operate from here. The bay itself is shallow, but the nearby Merkanti Reef (approximately 100 metres from shore) is a shallow horseshoe-shaped reef with vertical walls at around 10 metres depth, serving as the house reef for local operators. The urban location makes it one of Malta's most convenient dive sites for hotel-based divers.

## Site Information

- **Location**: St. Julian's/Paceville, NE Malta
- **Entry Type**: Shore entry
- **Site Type**: Coral reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Beginner
- **Maximum Depth**: 15 meters
- **Typical Visibility**: 10–20 meters
- **Current**: Light
- **Best Time**: May to October

## Marine Life

At nearby Merkanti Reef: wrasse, octopus, crabs, cuttlefish, and seahorses. The urban reef supports surprisingly diverse marine life.

## Tips and Recommendations

- Most convenient urban dive site in Malta — walk from your hotel
- Merkanti Reef (100m from shore) is the actual dive objective
- Multiple dive centres right on the beach
- Good for night diving
