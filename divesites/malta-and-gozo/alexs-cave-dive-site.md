## Alex's Cave (Dive Site)

Alex's Cave is a small cavern dive in the Comino Island area between Malta and Gozo. Named for an early local diving pioneer, it is one of several accessible cavern sites clustered around the Cominotto islet and the Blue Lagoon, making it a natural addition to any Comino dive day.

## Overview

The cave is a short, well-lit cavern accessible at a beginner-friendly 20 metres maximum depth. The cave entrance and interior receive natural light, giving the site the characteristic turquoise and blue Maltese cave ambiance. The limestone walls are encrusted with sponges, algae, and small invertebrates. The crystal-clear waters around Comino, regularly exceeding 20–25 metres visibility, make this an exceptionally beautiful location for cave introduction dives.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Comino Island area, Malta
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Cave/cavern
- **Difficulty Level**: Beginner
- **Maximum Depth**: 20 meters
- **Typical Visibility**: 20–30 meters
- **Current**: Generally calm; exposed to NW wind
- **Best Time**: Year-round (best April to November)

## Marine Life

The cave shelters small grouper, moray eels, and crabs in its recesses. The surrounding reef supports sea bream, ornate wrasse, and barracuda. Octopus are frequently seen in the rocky areas outside the cave entrance. The limestone reef surrounding Comino is home to a healthy Mediterranean fish community.

## Dive Profile

Descend from the boat to the cave entrance at 10–15 metres and enter the cavern, staying within the natural light zone. Explore the interior and exit, then spend time on the surrounding reef. The very shallow areas in the Blue Lagoon vicinity allow extended bottom time. Safety stop at 5 metres.

## Entry and Exit

Dive boats from Gozo or Malta operators serve Comino as a multi-site day trip. Entry by giant stride. The Comino area can be exposed to NW swells — check conditions before diving.

## Tips and Recommendations

- Ideal as an introduction to Maltese cave diving for Open Water certified divers
- Combine with the nearby Cominotto Arch and Crystal Lagoon for a comprehensive Comino programme
- Visibility around Comino is consistently among the best in the Mediterranean
- A torch illuminates the cave walls and brings out their vivid colours
- The Blue Lagoon above water is extremely busy with tourist boats in summer — arrive early

## Safety Considerations

Cavern principles apply: maintain orientation to the entrance. The 20-metre maximum depth is well within beginner limits. Always carry an SMB and dive with a buddy.

## Photography

The turquoise cave entrance framing the vivid blue Mediterranean water is a classic Maltese shot. The clear water makes natural light photography effective throughout the cavern. A torch adds colour to the encrusted interior surfaces.
