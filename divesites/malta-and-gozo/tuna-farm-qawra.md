# Tuna Farm (Qawra)

Dive inside a tuna pen surrounded by hundreds of massive bluefin tuna — a unique and unforgettable Malta experience.

## Overview

Tuna Farm (Qawra) offers a unique diving experience inside large circular net pens (50–90 metres diameter) containing hundreds of bluefin tuna. Divers descend into the pen and are surrounded by tuna ranging 1–2 metres long, some weighing up to 400–450 kg. The tuna swim in one direction creating a vortex that can pull divers deeper — depth monitoring is critical. There is no natural reef — the dive is entirely about the tuna encounter. Gear needs thorough cleaning afterward due to the smell. This is an offshore pen operation reached by boat charter.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Offshore, Qawra area
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Advanced
- **Maximum Depth**: 35 meters
- **Typical Visibility**: 15–30 meters
- **Current**: Light to moderate
- **Best Time**: May to October

## Marine Life

Hundreds of bluefin tuna per pen, ranging 1–2 metres long, some up to 400–450 kg. An extraordinary close encounter with one of the ocean's most powerful fish.

## Tips and Recommendations

- Check conditions with local operators before diving
- Carry a torch for overhangs and cave features
- Dive within your certification limits
