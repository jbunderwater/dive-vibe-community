# Tac-Cawla Rocks

The shallow beginner-friendly entry to the Tac-Cawla cave complex on Gozo's west coast.

## Overview

Tac-Cawla Rocks is the shallow reef and surface-level cave entrance area of the larger Tac-Cawla Caves complex on Gozo's west coast. While the main cave system (Tac-Cawla Caves) reaches 35 metres and requires experienced divers, this site covers the shallow reef and visible cave entrances at 5–10 metres depth, making it accessible to beginners. Shore access is possible but difficult.

## Site Information

- **Location**: West coast Gozo, Malta
- **Entry Type**: Shore entry
- **Site Type**: Cave/cavern
- **Difficulty Level**: Beginner
- **Maximum Depth**: 10 meters
- **Typical Visibility**: 20–30 meters
- **Current**: Light to moderate
- **Best Time**: May to October

## Tips and Recommendations

- Carry a torch for cave interiors
- Dive within your certification limits
- Check conditions with local operators before diving
