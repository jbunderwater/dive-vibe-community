# Tal-Barba Cave

A cave along Gozo's west coast cliff systems.

## Overview

Tal-Barba Cave is located along Gozo's west coast cliff systems. The cave sits at approximately 30 metres depth, part of the extensive network of sea caves that riddle the base of Gozo's western cliffs. The area features dramatic cliff-base topography with boulders, overhangs, and fissures typical of Gozo's Globigerina limestone coastline.
