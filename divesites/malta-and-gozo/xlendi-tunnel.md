# Xlendi Tunnel

A beautiful tunnel cave and reef at Xlendi Bay — scenic surroundings with a pinnacle and shallow swimthrough.

## Overview

Xlendi Tunnel is located on the northern wall of Xlendi Bay, Gozo. The site features a beautiful tunnel cave and reef with a pinnacle. A very shallow tunnel opens from the cave back, running through the headland. The scenic bay surroundings with varied underwater features make this popular for photography. The bay extends to 60+ metres depth on the outer wall.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Xlendi Bay, south coast Gozo
- **Entry Type**: Shore entry
- **Site Type**: Cave/cavern
- **Difficulty Level**: Beginner
- **Maximum Depth**: 10 meters
- **Typical Visibility**: 20–30 meters
- **Current**: Light to moderate
- **Best Time**: May to October

## Marine Life

Mediterranean reef species. The tunnel and reef support grouper, moray eels, and nudibranchs.

## Tips and Recommendations

- The shallow tunnel through the headland is the highlight
- Popular for underwater photography
- Xlendi Bay is a scenic starting point
- Can go very deep on the outer wall — watch your depth
