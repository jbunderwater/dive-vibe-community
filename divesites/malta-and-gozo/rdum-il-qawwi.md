# Rdum il-Qawwi

Numerous natural swim-throughs, arches, and caves created by immense boulders along the shoreline.

## Overview

Rdum il-Qawwi features numerous swim-throughs created by immense boulders along the shoreline on Gozo's coast. Arches and caves line the coast with various sponges and golden zoanthids throughout. Access requires a 500+ metre walk over steps and footpaths — better done from a boat. Wrasse inhabits the Posidonia seagrass fields surrounding the boulder formations.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Gozo coast, Malta
- **Entry Type**: Shore entry
- **Site Type**: Cave/cavern
- **Difficulty Level**: Beginner
- **Maximum Depth**: 6 meters
- **Typical Visibility**: 20–30 meters
- **Current**: Light to moderate
- **Best Time**: May to October

## Tips and Recommendations

- Carry a torch for cave interiors
- Dive within your certification limits
- Check conditions with local operators before diving
