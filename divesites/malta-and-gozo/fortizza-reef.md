# Fortizza Reef

Caverns, tunnels, arches, and overhangs off Sliema — rated 4/5 stars by divers.

## Overview

Fortizza Reef is part of the Sliema House Reef complex, featuring caverns, tunnels, arches, overhangs, valleys, and gullies at a maximum depth of 15 metres. Shore access is easy from the Sliema promenade. Rated 4/5 stars by divers, the site offers abundant swim-throughs and a diverse array of Mediterranean marine life.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Sliema promenade, Malta
- **Entry Type**: Shore entry
- **Site Type**: Reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Beginner
- **Maximum Depth**: 15 meters
- **Typical Visibility**: 15–30 meters
- **Current**: Light to moderate
- **Best Time**: May to October

## Marine Life

Nudibranchs, cuttlefish, octopus, moray eels, wrasse, and bream. The numerous caverns and overhangs shelter resident species.

## Tips and Recommendations

- Check conditions with local operators before diving
- Carry a torch for overhangs and cave features
- Dive within your certification limits
