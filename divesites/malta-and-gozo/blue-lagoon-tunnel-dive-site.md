# Blue Lagoon Tunnel Dive Site

An underwater tunnel connecting the Blue Lagoon area of Comino to deeper water.

## Overview

The Blue Lagoon Tunnel is part of the Comino dive site complex near the famous Blue Lagoon. The tunnel provides passage from the shallow lagoon area to deeper reef and wall sections. Comino's consistently excellent visibility (often exceeding 30 metres) makes this an atmospheric dive. The site is popular with snorkellers and divers alike.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Blue Lagoon, Comino
- **Entry Type**: Shore entry
- **Site Type**: Cave/cavern
- **Difficulty Level**: Beginner
- **Maximum Depth**: 12 meters
- **Typical Visibility**: 25–40 meters
- **Current**: Light to moderate
- **Best Time**: May to October

## Marine Life

Mediterranean reef species typical of the crystal-clear Comino waters.

## Tips and Recommendations

- Part of the famous Blue Lagoon area
- Comino has some of Malta's best visibility
