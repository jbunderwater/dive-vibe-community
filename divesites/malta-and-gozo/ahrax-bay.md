# Ahrax Bay

A rock canyon leading to an underwater cave and enclosed 'inland sea' — sunlight creates an enchanting light display.

## Overview

Ahrax Bay at L'Ahrax Point features a rock canyon leading to an underwater cave entrance at 9 metres depth, which opens into an enclosed inland sea with dramatic light effects. The canyon walls and rocky reef formations extend to approximately 21 metres depth. The underwater cave entrance leading to the closed inland sea is the main attraction — sunlight filtering through creates an enchanting light display.

## Site Information

- **Location**: L'Ahrax Point, north Malta
- **Entry Type**: Shore entry
- **Site Type**: Cave/cavern
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 20 meters
- **Typical Visibility**: 15–30 meters
- **Current**: Light to moderate
- **Best Time**: May to October

## Marine Life

The canyon walls and rocky reef support typical Mediterranean species. The cave interior hosts organisms adapted to low-light conditions.

## Tips and Recommendations

- Check conditions with local operators before diving
- Carry a torch for overhangs and cave features
- Dive within your certification limits
