# Coral Gardens

Shallow valleys, canyons, and swimthrough tunnels off Sliema — part of the House Reef complex and excellent for macro photography.

## Overview

Coral Gardens is a shallow reef site adjacent to Fortizza Reef, part of the Sliema House Reef complex. The site features valleys, canyons, gullies, rock formations, and swimthrough tunnels at a maximum depth of 15 metres. Shore access is easy, directly off the Sliema promenade. The site has 'lots of small marine life' making it excellent for macro photography.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Sliema promenade, Malta
- **Entry Type**: Shore entry
- **Site Type**: Reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Beginner
- **Maximum Depth**: 15 meters
- **Typical Visibility**: 15–30 meters
- **Current**: Light to moderate
- **Best Time**: May to October

## Marine Life

Abundant small marine life — excellent for macro photography. Nudibranchs, small crustaceans, and juvenile fish among the rock formations.

## Tips and Recommendations

- Check conditions with local operators before diving
- Carry a torch for overhangs and cave features
- Dive within your certification limits
