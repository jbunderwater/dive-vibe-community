# Whale Cave

A cave system on Gozo's west coast accessible from outside the Inland Sea tunnel.

## Overview

Whale Cave is located on Gozo's west coast, north of the seaward exit of the Inland Sea tunnel at Dwejra. It is part of the cave system that divers can explore after exiting the 80-metre Inland Sea tunnel into open water. The seabed in this area drops to approximately 50 metres, and the cave is accessible from the deeper sections outside the tunnel.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Dwejra, west coast Gozo (north of Inland Sea exit), Malta
- **Entry Type**: Shore entry
- **Site Type**: Cave/cavern
- **Difficulty Level**: Advanced
- **Maximum Depth**: 30 meters
- **Typical Visibility**: 20–30 meters
- **Current**: Light to moderate
- **Best Time**: May to October

## Tips and Recommendations

- Carry a torch for cave interiors
- Dive within your certification limits
- Check conditions with local operators before diving
