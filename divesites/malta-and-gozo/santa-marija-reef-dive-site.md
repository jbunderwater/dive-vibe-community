# Santa Marija Reef Dive Site

A reef dive near the Santa Marija area of Comino — crystal-clear waters and Mediterranean reef species.

## Overview

Santa Marija Reef is a reef dive site near the Santa Marija area of Comino island. The reef supports typical Mediterranean species in Comino's famously clear waters. The site can be combined with the nearby Santa Marija Caves for a varied dive day.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Comino island
- **Entry Type**: Boat dive
- **Site Type**: Coral reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
- **Maximum Depth**: 20 meters
- **Typical Visibility**: 25–40 meters
- **Current**: Light to moderate
- **Best Time**: May to October

## Marine Life

Mediterranean reef species in crystal-clear water. Bream, wrasse, and octopus are common.

## Tips and Recommendations

- Comino's clear waters provide exceptional visibility
- Combine with Santa Marija Caves nearby
