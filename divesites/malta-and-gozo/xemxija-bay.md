# Xemxija Bay

A sheltered training bay in the St. Paul's Bay area — used as a house reef by local operators.

## Overview

Xemxija Bay is a small sheltered bay within the St. Paul's Bay area on Malta's north coast, used primarily as a house reef by local dive operators for discover scuba diving courses and training dives. The bay features Posidonia seagrass meadows and rocky/sandy bottom typical of Maltese bays, with calm conditions suitable for beginners.

## Site Information

- **Location**: Xemxija, St. Paul's Bay, north Malta
- **Entry Type**: Shore entry
- **Site Type**: Coral reef
- **Difficulty Level**: Beginner
- **Maximum Depth**: 12 meters
- **Typical Visibility**: 10–20 meters
- **Current**: Light; sheltered
- **Best Time**: Year-round

## Marine Life

Typical Mediterranean bay species — wrasse, octopus, and small reef fish in the Posidonia habitat.

## Tips and Recommendations

- Primarily a training and discover scuba site
- Sheltered conditions year-round
- Multiple dive operators offer pickups from Xemxija
