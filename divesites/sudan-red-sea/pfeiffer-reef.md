## Pfeiffer Reef

Pfeiffer Reef is an offshore pinnacle reef roughly 2km southwest of Angarosh, visited on northern Sudan liveaboard itineraries.

## Overview

Pfeiffer Reef is an elongated reef rising from a seafloor roughly 450 metres deep, with a small plateau at its north end carrying a soft coral bommie — details confirmed by Dive The World's Sudan dive-site guide.
